*The Way of Love*
###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> If I speak in the tongues of men and of angels, but I do not have love, I am a noisy gong or a clanging cymbal. ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> And if I have prophecy, and know all mysteries and all knowledge, and if I have all faith so that I can remove mountains, but do not have love, I am nothing. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> If I give away everything I own, and if I give over my body in order to boast, <sup class="nt-tc">[[I Corinthians 13 — NET Notes#^n460131|‡]]</sup> but do not have love, I receive no benefit. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> Love is patient, love is kind, it is not envious. Love does not brag, it is not puffed up. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> It is not rude, it is not self-serving, it is not easily angered or resentful. ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> It is not glad about injustice, but rejoices in the truth. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> It bears all things, believes all things, hopes all things, endures all things. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Love never ends. But if there are prophecies, they will be set aside; if there are tongues, they will cease; if there is knowledge, it will be set aside. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> For we know in part, and we prophesy in part, ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> but when what is perfect <sup class="nt-tn">[[I Corinthians 13 — NET Notes#^n460132|*]]</sup> comes, the partial will be set aside. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> When I was a child, I talked like a child, I thought like a child, I reasoned like a child. But when I became an adult, <sup class="nt-tn">[[I Corinthians 13 — NET Notes#^n460133|*]]</sup> I set aside childish ways. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> For now we see in a mirror indirectly, <sup class="nt-tn">[[I Corinthians 13 — NET Notes#^n460134|*]]</sup> but then we will see face to face. Now I know in part, but then I will know fully, just as I have been fully known. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> And now these three remain: faith, hope, and love. But the greatest of these is love. ^v13
