*Introduction and Blessing*
###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> The Revelation of Jesus Christ, which God gave Him to show His servants—things which must shortly take place. And He sent and signified it by His angel to His servant John,<sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^f6053|†]]</sup> ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> who bore witness to the word of God, and to the testimony of Jesus Christ, to all things that he saw.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^f6054|†]]</sup> ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> Blessed is he who reads and those who hear the words of this prophecy, and keep those things which are written in it; for the time is near.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^f6055|†]]</sup> ^v3

*Greeting to the Seven Churches*
###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> John, to the seven churches which are in Asia: Grace to you and peace from Him who is and who was and who is to come, and from the seven Spirits who are before His throne,<sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^f6056|†]]</sup> ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> and from Jesus Christ, the faithful witness, the firstborn from the dead, and the ruler over the kings of the earth. To Him who loved us and washed<sup class="nt-tc">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar776|‡]]</sup> us from our sins in His own blood,<sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^f6057|†]]</sup> ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> and has made us kings<sup class="nt-tc">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar777|‡]]</sup> and priests to His God and Father, to Him be glory and dominion forever and ever. Amen.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^f6058|†]]</sup> ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> Behold, He is coming with clouds, and every eye will see Him, even they who pierced Him. And all the tribes of the earth will mourn because of Him. Even so, Amen. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> “I am the Alpha and the Omega, the Beginning and the End,”<sup class="nt-tc">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar778|‡]]</sup> says the Lord,<sup class="nt-tc">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar779|‡]]</sup> “who is and who was and who is to come, the Almighty.”<sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^f6059|†]]</sup> ^v8

*Vision and Commission of Christ*
###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> I, John, both<sup class="nt-tc">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar780|‡]]</sup> your brother and companion in the tribulation and kingdom and patience of Jesus Christ, was on the island that is called Patmos for the word of God and for the testimony of Jesus Christ.<sup class="nt-bg">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^fback1|◦]]</sup> ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> I was in the Spirit on the Lord's Day, and I heard behind me a loud voice, as of a trumpet,<sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^f6060|†]]</sup> ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> saying, “I am the Alpha and the Omega, the First and the Last,”<sup class="nt-tc">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar781|‡]]</sup> and, “What you see, write in a book and send it to the seven churches which are in Asia:<sup class="nt-tc">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar782|‡]]</sup> to Ephesus, to Smyrna, to Pergamos, to Thyatira, to Sardis, to Philadelphia, and to Laodicea.”<sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^f6061|†]]</sup> ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> Then I turned to see the voice that spoke with me. And having turned I saw seven golden lampstands, ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> and in the midst of the seven lampstands One like the Son of Man, clothed with a garment down to the feet and girded about the chest with a golden band.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^f6062|†]]</sup> ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> His head and hair were white like wool, as white as snow, and His eyes like a flame of fire;<sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^f6063|†]]</sup> ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> His feet were like fine brass, as if refined in a furnace, and His voice as the sound of many waters;<sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^f6064|†]]</sup> ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> He had in His right hand seven stars, out of His mouth went a sharp two-edged sword, and His countenance was like the sun shining in its strength.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^f6065|†]]</sup> ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> And when I saw Him, I fell at His feet as dead. But He laid His right hand on me, saying to me,<sup class="nt-tc">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar783|‡]]</sup> “Do not be afraid; I am the First and the Last.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^f6066|†]]</sup> ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> I am He who lives, and was dead, and behold, I am alive forevermore. Amen. And I have the keys of Hades and of Death. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> Write<sup class="nt-tc">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar784|‡]]</sup> the things which you have seen, and the things which are, and the things which will take place after this.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^f6067|†]]</sup> ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> The mystery of the seven stars which you saw in My right hand, and the seven golden lampstands: The seven stars are the angels of the seven churches, and the seven lampstands which you saw<sup class="nt-tc">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar785|‡]]</sup> are the seven churches.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — OSB Notes#^f6068|†]]</sup> ^v20
