*The Genealogy of Jesus Christ*
###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> This is the record of the genealogy <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n400011|*]]</sup> of Jesus Christ, the son of David, the son of Abraham. ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> Abraham was the father <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n400012|*]]</sup> of Isaac, Isaac the father of Jacob, Jacob the father of Judah and his brothers, ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> Judah the father of Perez and Zerah (by Tamar), Perez the father of Hezron, Hezron the father of Ram, ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> Ram the father of Amminadab, Amminadab the father of Nahshon, Nahshon the father of Salmon, ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> Salmon the father of Boaz (by Rahab), Boaz the father of Obed (by Ruth), Obed the father of Jesse, ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> and Jesse the father of David the king. David was the father of Solomon (by the wife of Uriah <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n400013|†]]</sup> ), ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> Solomon the father of Rehoboam, Rehoboam the father of Abijah, Abijah the father of Asa, <sup class="nt-tc">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n400014|‡]]</sup> ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Asa the father of Jehoshaphat, Jehoshaphat the father of Joram, Joram the father of Uzziah, ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> Uzziah the father of Jotham, Jotham the father of Ahaz, Ahaz the father of Hezekiah, ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> Hezekiah the father of Manasseh, Manasseh the father of Amon, <sup class="nt-tc">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n400015|‡]]</sup> Amon the father of Josiah, ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> and Josiah <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n400016|†]]</sup> the father of Jeconiah and his brothers, at the time of the deportation to Babylon. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> After <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n400017|*]]</sup> the deportation to Babylon, Jeconiah became the father of Shealtiel, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n400018|†]]</sup> Shealtiel the father of Zerubbabel, ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> Zerubbabel the father of Abiud, Abiud the father of Eliakim, Eliakim the father of Azor, ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> Azor the father of Zadok, Zadok the father of Achim, Achim the father of Eliud, ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> Eliud the father of Eleazar, Eleazar the father of Matthan, Matthan the father of Jacob, ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> and Jacob the father of Joseph, the husband of Mary, by whom <sup class="nt-tc">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n400019|‡]]</sup> Jesus was born, who is called Christ. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n4000110|*]]</sup> ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> So all the generations from Abraham to David are fourteen generations, and from David to the deportation to Babylon, fourteen generations, and from the deportation to Babylon to Christ, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n4000111|*]]</sup> fourteen generations. ^v17

*The Birth of Jesus Christ*
###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> Now the birth of Jesus Christ happened this way. While his mother Mary was engaged to Joseph, but before they came together, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n4000112|*]]</sup> she was found to be pregnant through the Holy Spirit. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> Because Joseph, her husband to be, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n4000113|*]]</sup> was a righteous man, and because he did not want to disgrace her, he intended to divorce her <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n4000114|*]]</sup> privately. ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> When he had contemplated this, an <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n4000115|*]]</sup> angel of the Lord <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n4000116|*]]</sup> appeared to him in a dream and said, “Joseph, son of David, do not be afraid to take Mary as your wife, because the child conceived in her is from the Holy Spirit. ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> She will give birth to a son and you will name him <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n4000117|*]]</sup> Jesus, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n4000118|†]]</sup> because he will save his people from their sins.” ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> This all happened so that what was spoken by the Lord through the prophet would be fulfilled: ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> “ Look! The virgin will conceive and give birth to a son, and they will name him <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n4000119|*]]</sup> Emmanuel,” <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n4000120|†]]</sup> which means <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n4000121|*]]</sup> “ God with us.” <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n4000122|†]]</sup> ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> When Joseph awoke from sleep he did what the angel of the Lord <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n4000123|*]]</sup> told him. He took his wife, ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> but did not have marital relations <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n4000124|*]]</sup> with her until she gave birth to a son, whom he named <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 1 — NET Notes#^n4000125|*]]</sup> Jesus. ^v25
