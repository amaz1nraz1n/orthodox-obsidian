###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> You will proclaim wisdom so that insight may obey you, ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> for she is upon the top of the heights, and she stands among the worn paths; ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> for she sits beside the gates of rulers, and in the entrances she sings: ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> “O people, I summon you, and I utter my voice to the sons of humans. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> Naive people, perceive cunning, and foolish people, take it to heart! ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> Listen to me; for I will say serious things, and I will bring straight things up from my lips; ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> because my throat will meditate on truth, but false lips are repulsive before me. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> All the words of my mouth are in righteousness; nothing in them is crooked or twisted. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> Everything is before those with understanding and is upright for those who find knowledge. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> Acquire instruction and not silver, and knowledge rather than refined gold; ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> for wisdom is better than costly stones, and all that is precious is not worth as much as it. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> I, wisdom, dwell with counsel, and I call upon knowledge and insight; for wisdom is better than costly stones. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> The fear of the Lord hates unrighteousness, and also insolence and pride and ways of the wicked; and I hate distorted ways of evil people. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> Mine are counsel and security; mine is insight and mine is strength; ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> by me kings reign, and the rulers write righteousness; ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> by me mighty men become mighty, and tyrants control the earth by me. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> I love those who regard me with affection, and those who seek me will find me. ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> Wealth and glory belong to me, and the possessions of many and righteousness; ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> enjoyment of my fruits is better than gold or a precious stone, and my products are better than choicest silver. ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> I walk in ways of righteousness, and among paths of duties I conduct myself, ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> so that I will divide possessions to those who love me, and I will fill their storehouses with good things. If I report to you what happens every day, I will remember to number the events from eternity. ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> The Lord created me as the beginning of his ways for his works; ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> before eternity he founded me in the beginning, before making the earth ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> and before making the depths, before the springs of the waters came forth, ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> before the mountains were settled, and before all the hills, he produced me. ^v25

###### v26
<span class="vn">26</span> The Lord made territories and uninhabited regions and inhabited extremities of the earth under the skies. ^v26

###### v27
<span class="vn">27</span> When he was preparing the heavens, I was there with him, and when he was marking off his own throne upon the winds. ^v27

###### v28
<span class="vn">28</span> And as he was making the clouds above strong, and as he was establishing the unfailing springs of the earth under the heavens, ^v28

###### v29
<span class="vn">29</span> and as he was making the foundations of the earth strong, ^v29

###### v30
<span class="vn">30</span> I was beside him joining it; I was what he delighted in, and every day I rejoiced in his face at all times, ^v30

###### v31
<span class="vn">31</span> when he rejoiced upon completing the inhabited earth, and he rejoiced in the sons of humans. ^v31

###### v32
<span class="vn">32</span> Now therefore, son, listen to me. ^v32

###### v34
<span class="vn">34</span> Happy is the person who will listen to me, and the man who will guard my ways, watching at my doors each day, keeping watch over the doorposts of my entrances. ^v34

###### v35
<span class="vn">35</span> For my courses are the courses of life, and favor is prepared from the Lord. ^v35

###### v36
<span class="vn">36</span> And those who sin against me act profanely toward their own souls, and those who hate me love death.” ^v36
