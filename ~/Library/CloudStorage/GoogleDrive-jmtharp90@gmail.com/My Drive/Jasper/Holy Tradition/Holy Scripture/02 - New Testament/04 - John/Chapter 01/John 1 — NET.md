<span class="vn">15</span> John <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — NET Notes#^n4300139|†]]</sup> testified <sup class="nt-tn">[[John 1 — NET Notes#^n4300140|*]]</sup> about him and shouted out, <sup class="nt-tn">[[John 1 — NET Notes#^n4300141|*]]</sup> “This one was the one about whom I said, ‘He who comes after me is greater than I am, <sup class="nt-tn">[[John 1 — NET Notes#^n4300142|*]]</sup> because he existed before me.’” ^v15

###### v30
<span class="vn">30</span> This is the one about whom I said, ‘After me comes a man who is greater than I am, <sup class="nt-tn">[[John 1 — NET Notes#^n4300177|*]]</sup> because he existed before me.’ ^v30
