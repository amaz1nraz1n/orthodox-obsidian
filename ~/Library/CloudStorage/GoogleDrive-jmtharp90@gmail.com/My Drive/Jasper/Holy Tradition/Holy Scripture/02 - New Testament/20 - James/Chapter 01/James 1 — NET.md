*Salutation*
###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> From James, <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n590011|*]]</sup> a slave <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n590012|*]]</sup> of God and the Lord Jesus Christ, to the twelve tribes dispersed abroad. <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n590013|*]]</sup> Greetings! ^v1

*Joy in Trials*
###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> My brothers and sisters, <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n590014|*]]</sup> consider it nothing but joy <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n590015|*]]</sup> when you fall into all sorts of trials, ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> because you know that the testing of your faith produces endurance. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> And let endurance have its perfect effect, so that you will be perfect and complete, not deficient in anything. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> But if anyone is deficient in wisdom, he should ask God, who gives to all generously and without reprimand, and it will be given to him. ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> But he must ask in faith without doubting, for the one who doubts is like a wave of the sea, blown and tossed around by the wind. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> For that person must not suppose that he will receive anything from the Lord, ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> since he is a double-minded individual, <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n590016|*]]</sup> unstable in all his ways. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> Now the believer <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n590017|*]]</sup> of humble means <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n590018|*]]</sup> should take pride <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n590019|*]]</sup> in his high position. <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900110|*]]</sup> ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> But the rich person’s pride should be in his humiliation, because he will pass away like a wildflower in the meadow. <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900111|*]]</sup> ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> For the sun rises with its heat and dries up the meadow; the petal of the flower falls off and its beauty is lost forever. <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900112|*]]</sup> So also the rich person in the midst of his pursuits will wither away. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> Happy is the one <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900113|*]]</sup> who endures testing, because when he has proven to be genuine, he will receive the crown of life that God <sup class="nt-tc">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900114|‡]]</sup> promised to those who love him. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> Let no one say when he is tempted, “I am tempted by God,” for God cannot be tempted by evil, <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900115|*]]</sup> and he himself tempts no one. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> But each one is tempted when he is lured and enticed by his own desires. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> Then when desire conceives, it gives birth to sin, and when sin is full grown, it gives birth to death. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> Do not be led astray, my dear brothers and sisters. <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900116|*]]</sup> ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> All generous giving and every perfect gift <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900117|*]]</sup> is from above, coming down <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900118|*]]</sup> from the Father of lights, with whom there is no variation or the slightest hint of change. <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900119|*]]</sup> ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> By his sovereign plan he gave us birth <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900120|*]]</sup> through the message of truth, that we would be a kind of firstfruits of all he created. ^v18

*Living Out the Message*
###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> Understand this, my dear brothers and sisters! <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900121|*]]</sup> Let every person be quick to listen, slow to speak, slow to anger. ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> For human <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900122|*]]</sup> anger does not accomplish God’s righteousness. <sup class="nt-fn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900123|†]]</sup> ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> So put away all filth and evil excess and humbly <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900124|*]]</sup> welcome the message implanted within you, which is able to save your souls. ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> But be sure you live out the message and do not merely listen to it and so deceive yourselves. ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> For if someone merely listens to the message and does not live it out, he is like someone <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900125|*]]</sup> who gazes at his own face <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900126|*]]</sup> in a mirror. ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> For he gazes at himself and then goes out and immediately forgets <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900127|*]]</sup> what sort of person he was. ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> But the one who peers into the perfect law of liberty and fixes his attention there, <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900128|*]]</sup> and does not become a forgetful listener but one who lives it out—he <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900129|*]]</sup> will be blessed in what he does. <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900130|*]]</sup> ^v25

###### v26
<span class="vn">26</span> If someone thinks he is religious yet does not bridle his tongue, and so deceives his heart, his religion is futile. ^v26

###### v27
<span class="vn">27</span> Pure and undefiled religion before <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900131|*]]</sup> God the Father <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900132|*]]</sup> is this: to care for orphans and widows in their adversity <sup class="nt-tn">[[James 1 — NET Notes#^n5900133|*]]</sup> and to keep oneself unstained by the world. ^v27
