###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> The word of God that came to Jeremiah, the son of Hilkiah, one of the priests, who dwelled in Anathoth in the land of Benjamin; ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> the word of God that came to him in the days of Josiah, son of Amos, the king of Judah, in the thirteenth year of his kingdom. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> And it happened in the days of Jehoiakim, son of Josiah, the king of Judah, until the eleventh year of Zedekiah, son of Josiah, the king of Judah, until the captivity of Jerusalem in the fifth month. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> And the word of the Lord came to him: ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> “I knew you before I formed you in the belly, and I sanctified you before you came out; I established you as a prophet to the nations.” ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> And I said, “Lord who is master, look, I do not know what to say, because I am a youth.” ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> And the Lord said to me, “Do not say, ‘I am a youth,’ for you will go to anyone to whom I send you, and you will speak anything I command you. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Do not be afraid of their face, for I am with you to deliver you,” says the Lord. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> And the Lord stretched out his hand toward me and touched my mouth. And the Lord said to me, “Look, I have placed my words into your mouth. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> Look, I have appointed you today to nations and kingdoms, in order to root out and cast down and utterly destroy and build up and plant.” ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> And the word of the Lord came to me, saying: “What do you see?” And I said, “An almond staff.” ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> And the Lord said to me, “You have seen rightly, because I have stirred up over my words in order to do them.” ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> And the word of the Lord came to me a second time, saying, “What do you see?” And I said, “A burning cauldron, and its face is from the face of the north.” ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> And the Lord said to me, “From the face of the north evils shall be kindled upon all who dwell in the land.” ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> “For look, I am convening all the kingdoms of the earth from the north,” says the Lord. “And they will come, and each one will set his throne against the entrance of the gates of Jerusalem and against all the walls that surround it and against all the cities of Judah. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> And I will speak to them in judgment concerning all their evil, seeing as they have forsaken me and have sacrificed to foreign gods and have bowed down to the works of their hands. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> And, you, gird up your loins and stand up and say anything I command you. Do not be afraid of their face or be terrified before them, for I am with you to deliver you,” says the Lord. ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> “Look, I have established you today as a firm city, and I have fortified all the kings of Judah, its rulers, and the people of the land as a brazen wall. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> And they will fight against you, and they shall not prevail against you, because I am with you to deliver you,” said the Lord. ^v19
