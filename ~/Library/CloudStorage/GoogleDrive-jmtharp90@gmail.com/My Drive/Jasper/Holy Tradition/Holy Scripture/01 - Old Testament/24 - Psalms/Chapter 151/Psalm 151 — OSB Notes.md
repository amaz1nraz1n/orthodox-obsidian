### [[Psalm 151#v1|151:1]]
^v1
> [!note] 151:1 ^f2128
> [[Psalms 151#v1|Ps 151]] is **outside the number** of the Psalter, but **written with David's own hand** (v. [[Psalms 151#v1|1]]). It concerns the slaying of the giant Goliath. Historically, David slew him as recorded in [[I Kingdoms 17#v1|1Kg 17]]. He slew him through humility (**I was small**, v. [[Psalms 151#v1|1]]) and through the Lord (v. [[Psalms 151#v3|3]]).
>
> From a spiritual standpoint, Goliath stands for the sinful passions of arrogance and vainglory (see also [[Psalms 143#v1|Ps 143]]). Thus, with the Lord's help, we slay these giants with humility.
