###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> Though I speak with the tongues of men and of angels, but have not love, I have become sounding brass or a clanging cymbal.<sup class="nt-fn">[[I Corinthians 13 — OSB Notes#^f5453|†]]</sup> ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> And though I have the gift of prophecy, and understand all mysteries and all knowledge, and though I have all faith, so that I could remove mountains, but have not love, I am nothing.<sup class="nt-fn">[[I Corinthians 13 — OSB Notes#^f5454|†]]</sup> ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> And though I bestow all my goods to feed the poor, and though I give my body to be burned,<sup class="nt-tc">[[I Corinthians 13 — OSB Notes#^fvar558|‡]]</sup> but have not love, it profits me nothing.<sup class="nt-fn">[[I Corinthians 13 — OSB Notes#^f5455|†]]</sup> ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> Love suffers long and is kind; love does not envy; love does not parade itself, is not puffed up;<sup class="nt-fn">[[I Corinthians 13 — OSB Notes#^f5456|†]]</sup> ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> does not behave rudely, does not seek its own, is not provoked, thinks no evil; ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> does not rejoice in iniquity, but rejoices in the truth; ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> bears all things, believes all things, hopes all things, endures all things. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Love never fails. But whether there are prophecies, they will fail; whether there are tongues, they will cease; whether there is knowledge, it will vanish away.<sup class="nt-fn">[[I Corinthians 13 — OSB Notes#^f5457|†]]</sup> ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> For we know in part and we prophesy in part. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> But when that which is perfect has come, then that which is in part will be done away. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> When I was a child, I spoke as a child, I understood as a child, I thought as a child; but when I became a man, I put away childish things.<sup class="nt-fn">[[I Corinthians 13 — OSB Notes#^f5458|†]]</sup> ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> For now we see in a mirror, dimly, but then face to face. Now I know in part, but then I shall know just as I also am known. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> And now abide faith, hope, love, these three; but the greatest of these is love. ^v13
