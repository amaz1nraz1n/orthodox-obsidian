###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> Blessed is the man who does not go in the counsel of the ungodly, and does not stand in the way of sinners, and does not sit in t he seat of evil persons. ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> But rather his will is focused on the law of the Lord, and in his law he will meditate day and night. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> He will be like the tree that has been planted along the streams of the rivers, which will give its fruit in its season. And its leaf will not fall off. In all things, however much he does, he will be given prosperity. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> Not so the ungodly, not so! But rather, they are like the chaff that the wind spreads abroad away from the face of the earth. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> On this account the ungodly will not stand up in the judgment, nor will the sinful stand in the council of the righteous, ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> because the Lord knows the way of the righteous; but the way of the ungodly will perish. ^v6
