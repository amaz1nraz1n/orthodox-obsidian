> **Nav:** [[Exodus 20|Hub]] · [[Exodus 20 — Lexham Notes|Lexham Notes]] · [[Exodus 20 — NET Notes|NET Notes]]

###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> And the Lord spoke all these words, saying, ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> “I am the Lord your God, who brought you out from the land of Egypt, out of the house of slavery. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> “You shall have no other gods except me. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> “You shall make for yourself no idol or likeness of anything in the heavens above and in the land below and in the waters under the land. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> You shall not bow down to them or serve them. For I, the Lord your God, am a jealous God, repaying the sin of the fathers upon the children unto the third and the fourth generation to those who hate me, ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> and showing mercy to thousands for those who love me and keep my commandments. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> “You shall not take the name of the Lord your God in vain. For the Lord your God will not acquit the one who takes his name in vain. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> “Remember the Sabbath day, to make it holy. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> Six days you shall work and do all your work. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> On the seventh day, there is a Sabbath to the Lord your God. You shall not perform any labor on it, you and your son and your daughter, your male servant and your maidservant, your ox and your beast of burden and any animal of yours, not even a sojourner who is sojourning among you. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> For in six days the Lord made the heavens and the earth and everything in them, and he rested on the seventh day. Because of this the Lord blessed the seventh day and made it holy. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> “Honor your father and mother, so that it may be well for you and so that you may be long-lived upon the good land that the Lord your God is giving to you. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> <sup class="nt-tn">[[Exodus 20 — Lexham Notes#^v13|*]]</sup> “You shall not commit adultery. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> “You shall not steal. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> “You shall not murder. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> “You shall not testify falsely against your neighbor with a false testimony. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> “You shall not desire the wife of your neighbor; you shall not desire the house of your neighbor, or his field or his male servant nor his female servant or his ox or his beast of burden, or any animal of his, or anything that belongs to your neighbor.” ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> And all the people were seeing the thunder and the lightning and the sound of the trumpet and the smoking mountain, and all the people stood back terrified at a distance. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> And they said to Moses, “You yourself speak to us, but do not let God speak to us, so we do not die!” ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> And Moses said to them, “Take courage! For God has come to you to test you, in order that his fear may be among you, so that you do not sin.” ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> The people stood at a distance, and Moses entered into the thick cloud where God was. ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> The Lord said to Moses, “This is what you shall say to the house of Jacob and announce to the sons of Israel: ‘You yourselves have seen that I have spoken with you from the heavens. ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> You shall not make for yourselves gods of silver, and you shall not make for yourselves gods of gold. ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> “ ‘You shall make an altar out of earth for me, and upon it you shall sacrifice your whole burnt offerings and your peace offerings and the sheep and your young bulls in every location where I may place my name,<sup class="nt-alt">[[Exodus 20 — Lexham Notes#^v24|⁺]]</sup> and I will be there with you, and I will bless you. ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> And if you make an altar for me out of stones, you shall not construct them using dressed stones, for you have used your hand tool on them and they are defiled. ^v25

###### v26
<span class="vn">26</span> You shall not go up to my altar by steps, so that you will not uncover your shame upon it.’ ” ^v26
