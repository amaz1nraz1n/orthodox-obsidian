###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> If I speak with the tongues of men and angels, but do not have love, I have become a noisy gong or a clanging cymbal. ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> If I have the gift of prophecy and know all mysteries and have all knowledge, and if I have all faith, so as to move mountains, but I do not have love, I am nothing. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> If I give out all my goods to feed the poor, and give my body so that I will burn <sup class="nt-fn">[[I Corinthians 13 — EOB Notes#^edn1800|†]]</sup> but do not have love, it profits me nothing. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> Love is patient and is kind; love does not envy. Love does not brag, it is not proud, ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> it does not behave inappropriately, it is not self-oriented. <sup class="nt-fn">[[I Corinthians 13 — EOB Notes#^edn1801|†]]</sup> [Love] does not take offence and does not keep track of evil, ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> it does not rejoice in unrighteousness, but rejoices with the truth. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> [Love] bears all things, believes all things, hopes all things, endures all things. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Love never fails. But as for prophecies, they will come to an end; as for tongues, they will cease; as for knowledge, it will pass away. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> We know [only] in part, and we prophesy [only] in part; ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> but when that which is complete comes, then that which is incomplete will be done away with. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> When I was a child, I spoke as a child, I felt as a child, I thought as a child. Now that I have become full-grown, I have put away childish things. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> Now indeed, we see in a mirror, dimly, <sup class="nt-fn">[[I Corinthians 13 — EOB Notes#^edn1802|†]]</sup> but then we shall see face to face. Now I know [only] in part, but then, I will know fully, even as I have been fully known. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> But now, faith, hope, and love remain: these three, and the greatest of these is love. ^v13
