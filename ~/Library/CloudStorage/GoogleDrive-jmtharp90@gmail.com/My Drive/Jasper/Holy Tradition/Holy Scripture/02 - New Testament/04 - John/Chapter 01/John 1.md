*The Baptist's Witness to the Word*
###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> There was a man sent from God, whose name was John.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^f4710|†]]</sup> ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> This man came for a witness, to bear witness of the Light, that all through him might believe. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> He was not that Light, but was sent to bear witness of that Light. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> That was the true Light which gives light to every man coming into the world.<sup class="nt-tc">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar321|‡]]</sup><sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^f4711|†]]</sup> ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> He was in the world, and the world was made through Him, and the world did not know Him. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> He came to His own,<sup class="nt-cross">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^fcross123|§]]</sup> and His own<sup class="nt-cross">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^fcross124|§]]</sup> did not receive Him. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> But as many as received Him, to them He gave the right to become children of God, to those who believe in His name:<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^f4712|†]]</sup> ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> who were born, not of blood, nor of the will of the flesh, nor of the will of man, but of God.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^f4713|†]]</sup> ^v13

*The Word Becomes Flesh to Reveal the Father*
###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> And the Word became flesh and dwelt among us, and we beheld His glory, the glory as of the only begotten of the Father, full of grace and truth.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^f4714|†]]</sup> ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> John bore witness of Him and cried out, saying, “This was He of whom I said, ‘He who comes after me is preferred before me, for He was before me.’ ” ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> And<sup class="nt-tc">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar322|‡]]</sup> of His fullness we have all received, and grace for grace.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^f4715|†]]</sup> ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> For the law was given through Moses, but grace and truth came through Jesus Christ. ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> No one has seen God at any time. The only begotten Son,<sup class="nt-tc">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar323|‡]]</sup> who is in the bosom of the Father, He has declared Him.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^f4716|†]]</sup> ^v18

*Who Is John the Baptist?*
###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> Now this is the testimony of John, when the Jews sent priests and Levites from Jerusalem to ask him, “Who are you?”<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^f4717|†]]</sup> ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> He confessed, and did not deny, but confessed, “I am not the Christ.” ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> And they asked him, “What then? Are you Elijah?” He said, “I am not.” “Are you the Prophet?” And he answered, “No.”<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^f4718|†]]</sup> ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> Then they said to him, “Who are you, that we may give an answer to those who sent us? What do you say about yourself?” ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> He said: “I am ‘The voice of one crying in the wilderness: “Make straight the way of the Lord,” ’<sup class="nt-cross">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^fcross125|§]]</sup> as the prophet Isaiah said.” ^v23

*Why Does John Baptize?*
###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> Now those who were sent were from the Pharisees. ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> And they asked him, saying, “Why then do you baptize if you are not the Christ, nor Elijah, nor the Prophet?” ^v25

###### v26
<span class="vn">26</span> John answered them, saying, “I baptize with water, but there stands One among you whom you do not know.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^f4719|†]]</sup> ^v26

###### v27
<span class="vn">27</span> It is He who, coming after me, is preferred before me, whose sandal strap I am not worthy to loose.” ^v27

###### v28
<span class="vn">28</span> These things were done in Bethabara<sup class="nt-tc">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar324|‡]]</sup> beyond the Jordan, where John was baptizing. ^v28

*The Baptist's Testimony to Christ*
###### v29
<span class="vn">29</span> The next day John saw Jesus coming toward him, and said, “Behold! The Lamb of God who takes away the sin of the world!<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^f4720|†]]</sup><sup class="nt-lit">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^fx284|☩]]</sup> ^v29

###### v30
<span class="vn">30</span> This is He of whom I said, ‘After me comes a Man who is preferred before me, for He was before me.’ ^v30

###### v31
<span class="vn">31</span> I did not know Him; but that He should be revealed to Israel, therefore I came baptizing with water.” ^v31

###### v32
<span class="vn">32</span> And John bore witness, saying, “I saw the Spirit descending from heaven like a dove, and He remained upon Him.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^f4721|†]]</sup> ^v32

###### v33
<span class="vn">33</span> I did not know Him, but He who sent me to baptize with water said to me, ‘Upon whom you see the Spirit descending, and remaining on Him, this is He who baptizes with the Holy Spirit.’ ^v33

###### v34
<span class="vn">34</span> And I have seen and testified that this is the Son of God.” ^v34

*The Baptist's Disciples Follow Jesus*
###### v35
<span class="vn">35</span> Again, the next day, John stood with two of his disciples.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^f4722|†]]</sup><sup class="nt-lit">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^fx285|☩]]</sup> ^v35

###### v36
<span class="vn">36</span> And looking at Jesus as He walked, he said, “Behold the Lamb of God!” ^v36

###### v37
<span class="vn">37</span> The two disciples heard him speak, and they followed Jesus. ^v37

###### v38
<span class="vn">38</span> Then Jesus turned, and seeing them following, said to them, “What do you seek?” They said to Him, “Rabbi” (which is to say, when translated, Teacher), “where are You staying?” ^v38

###### v39
<span class="vn">39</span> He said to them, “Come and see.” They came and saw where He was staying, and remained with Him that day (now it was about the tenth hour). ^v39

###### v40
<span class="vn">40</span> One of the two who heard John speak, and followed Him, was Andrew, Simon Peter's brother. ^v40

###### v41
<span class="vn">41</span> He first found his own brother Simon, and said to him, “We have found the Messiah” (which is translated, the Christ). ^v41

###### v42
<span class="vn">42</span> And he brought him to Jesus. Now when Jesus looked at him, He said, “You are Simon the son of Jonah.<sup class="nt-tc">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar325|‡]]</sup> You shall be called Cephas” (which is translated, A Stone). ^v42

###### v43
<span class="vn">43</span> The following day Jesus wanted to go to Galilee, and He found Philip and said to him, “Follow Me.”<sup class="nt-lit">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^fx286|☩]]</sup> ^v43

###### v44
<span class="vn">44</span> Now Philip was from Bethsaida, the city of Andrew and Peter. ^v44

###### v45
<span class="vn">45</span> Philip found Nathanael and said to him, “We have found Him of whom Moses in the law, and also the prophets, wrote—Jesus of Nazareth, the son of Joseph.” ^v45

###### v46
<span class="vn">46</span> And Nathanael said to him, “Can anything good come out of Nazareth?” Philip said to him, “Come and see.” ^v46

###### v47
<span class="vn">47</span> Jesus saw Nathanael coming toward Him, and said of him, “Behold, an Israelite indeed, in whom is no deceit!”<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^f4723|†]]</sup> ^v47

###### v48
<span class="vn">48</span> Nathanael said to Him, “How do You know me?” Jesus answered and said to him, “Before Philip called you, when you were under the fig tree, I saw you.”<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^f4724|†]]</sup> ^v48

###### v49
<span class="vn">49</span> Nathanael answered and said to Him, “Rabbi, You are the Son of God! You are the King of Israel!” ^v49

###### v50
<span class="vn">50</span> Jesus answered and said to him, “Because I said to you, ‘I saw you under the fig tree,’ do you believe? You will see greater things than these.” ^v50

###### v51
<span class="vn">51</span> And He said to him, “Most assuredly, I say to you, hereafter<sup class="nt-tc">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar326|‡]]</sup> you shall see heaven open, and the angels of God ascending and descending upon the Son of Man.”<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — OSB Notes#^f4725|†]]</sup> ^v51
