*The Beatitudes: The Blessings of True Discipleship*
###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> And seeing the multitudes, He went up on a mountain, and when He was seated His disciples came to Him.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4120|†]]</sup> ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> Then He opened His mouth and taught them, saying: ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> “Blessed are the poor in spirit, For theirs is the kingdom of heaven.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4121|†]]</sup> ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> Blessed are those who mourn, For they shall be comforted.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4122|†]]</sup> ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> Blessed are the meek, For they shall inherit the earth.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4123|†]]</sup> ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> Blessed are those who hunger and thirst for righteousness, For they shall be filled.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4124|†]]</sup> ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> Blessed are the merciful, For they shall obtain mercy.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4125|†]]</sup> ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Blessed are the pure in heart, For they shall see God.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4126|†]]</sup> ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> Blessed are the peacemakers, For they shall be called sons of God.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4127|†]]</sup> ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> Blessed are those who are persecuted for righteousness' sake, For theirs is the kingdom of heaven.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4128|†]]</sup> ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> “Blessed are you when they revile and persecute you, and say all kinds of evil against you falsely for My sake.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4129|†]]</sup> ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> Rejoice and be exceedingly glad, for great is your reward in heaven, for so they persecuted the prophets who were before you. ^v12

*The Disciples in the World*
###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> “You are the salt of the earth; but if the salt loses its flavor, how shall it be seasoned? It is then good for nothing but to be thrown out and trampled underfoot by men.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4130|†]]</sup> ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> “You are the light of the world. A city that is set on a hill cannot be hidden.<sup class="nt-lit">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^fx225|☩]]</sup> ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> Nor do they light a lamp and put it under a basket, but on a lampstand, and it gives light to all who are in the house. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> Let your light so shine before men, that they may see your good works and glorify your Father in heaven.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4131|†]]</sup> ^v16

*Righteousness: The Law Fulfilled*
###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> “Do not think that I came to destroy the Law or the Prophets. I did not come to destroy but to fulfill.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4132|†]]</sup> ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> For assuredly, I say to you, till heaven and earth pass away, one jot or one tittle will by no means pass from the law till all is fulfilled.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4133|†]]</sup> ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> Whoever therefore breaks one of the least of these commandments, and teaches men so, shall be called least in the kingdom of heaven; but whoever does and teaches them, he shall be called great in the kingdom of heaven.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4134|†]]</sup> ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> For I say to you, that unless your righteousness exceeds the righteousness of the scribes and Pharisees, you will by no means enter the kingdom of heaven.<sup class="nt-cit">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^fcit39|¶]]</sup> ^v20

*Righteousness Illustrated*
###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> “You have heard that it was said to those of old, ‘You shall not murder,<sup class="nt-cross">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^fcross12|§]]</sup> and whoever murders will be in danger of the judgment.’<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4135|†]]</sup> ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> But I say to you that whoever is angry with his brother without a cause<sup class="nt-tc">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^fvar7|‡]]</sup> shall be in danger of the judgment. And whoever says to his brother, ‘Raca!’ shall be in danger of the council. But whoever says, ‘You fool!’ shall be in danger of hell fire. ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> Therefore if you bring your gift to the altar, and there remember that your brother has something against you,<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4136|†]]</sup> ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> leave your gift there before the altar, and go your way. First be reconciled to your brother, and then come and offer your gift. ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> Agree with your adversary quickly, while you are on the way with him, lest your adversary deliver you to the judge, the judge hand you over to the officer, and you be thrown into prison.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4137|†]]</sup> ^v25

###### v26
<span class="vn">26</span> Assuredly, I say to you, you will by no means get out of there till you have paid the last penny. ^v26

###### v27
<span class="vn">27</span> “You have heard that it was said to those of old,<sup class="nt-tc">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^fvar8|‡]]</sup> ‘You shall not commit adultery.’<sup class="nt-cross">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^fcross13|§]]</sup><sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4138|†]]</sup> ^v27

###### v28
<span class="vn">28</span> But I say to you that whoever looks at a woman to lust for her has already committed adultery with her in his heart. ^v28

###### v29
<span class="vn">29</span> If your right eye causes you to sin, pluck it out and cast it from you; for it is more profitable for you that one of your members perish, than for your whole body to be cast into hell.<sup class="nt-cit">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^fcit40|¶]]</sup> ^v29

###### v30
<span class="vn">30</span> And if your right hand causes you to sin, cut it off and cast it from you; for it is more profitable for you that one of your members perish, than for your whole body to be cast into hell. ^v30

###### v31
<span class="vn">31</span> “Furthermore it has been said, ‘Whoever divorces his wife, let him give her a certificate of divorce.’<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4139|†]]</sup> ^v31

###### v32
<span class="vn">32</span> But I say to you that whoever divorces his wife for any reason except sexual immorality<sup class="nt-tc">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^fvar9|‡]]</sup> causes her to commit adultery; and whoever marries a woman who is divorced commits adultery. ^v32

###### v33
<span class="vn">33</span> “Again you have heard that it was said to those of old, ‘You shall not swear falsely, but shall perform your oaths to the Lord.’ ^v33

###### v34
<span class="vn">34</span> But I say to you, do not swear at all: neither by heaven, for it is God's throne;<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4140|†]]</sup> ^v34

###### v35
<span class="vn">35</span> nor by the earth, for it is His footstool; nor by Jerusalem, for it is the city of the great King. ^v35

###### v36
<span class="vn">36</span> Nor shall you swear by your head, because you cannot make one hair white or black. ^v36

###### v37
<span class="vn">37</span> But let your ‘Yes’ be ‘Yes,’ and your ‘No,’ ‘No.’ For whatever is more than these is from the evil one. ^v37

###### v38
<span class="vn">38</span> “You have heard that it was said, ‘An eye for an eye and a tooth for a tooth.’<sup class="nt-cross">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^fcross14|§]]</sup> ^v38

###### v39
<span class="vn">39</span> But I tell you not to resist an evil person. But whoever slaps you on your right cheek, turn the other to him also.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4141|†]]</sup> ^v39

###### v40
<span class="vn">40</span> If anyone wants to sue you and take away your tunic, let him have your cloak also. ^v40

###### v41
<span class="vn">41</span> And whoever compels you to go one mile, go with him two. ^v41

###### v42
<span class="vn">42</span> Give to him who asks you, and from him who wants to borrow from you do not turn away. ^v42

###### v43
<span class="vn">43</span> “You have heard that it was said, ‘You shall love your neighbor<sup class="nt-cross">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^fcross15|§]]</sup> and hate your enemy.’ ^v43

###### v44
<span class="vn">44</span> But I say to you, love your enemies, bless those who curse you, do good to those who hate you, and pray for those who spitefully use you and persecute you,<sup class="nt-tc">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^fvar10|‡]]</sup><sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4142|†]]</sup> ^v44

###### v45
<span class="vn">45</span> that you may be sons of your Father in heaven; for He makes His sun rise on the evil and on the good, and sends rain on the just and on the unjust. ^v45

###### v46
<span class="vn">46</span> For if you love those who love you, what reward have you? Do not even the tax collectors do the same? ^v46

###### v47
<span class="vn">47</span> And if you greet your brethren<sup class="nt-tc">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^fvar11|‡]]</sup> only, what do you do more than others? Do not even the tax collectors<sup class="nt-tc">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^fvar12|‡]]</sup> do so? ^v47

###### v48
<span class="vn">48</span> Therefore you shall be perfect, just as your Father in heaven is perfect.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — OSB Notes#^f4143|†]]</sup> ^v48
