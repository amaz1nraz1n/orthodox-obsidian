*The Tenacious Widow*

###### v19

<span class="vn">20</span> You know the commandments: ‘Do not commit adultery,’ ‘Do not murder,’ ‘Do not steal,’ ‘Do not bear false witness,’ ‘Honor your father and your mother.’ ”<sup class="nt-cross">[[Luke 18 — OSB Notes#^fcross108|§]]</sup> ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> And he said, “All these things I have kept from my youth.” ^v21

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> For it is easier for a camel to go through the eye of a needle than for a rich man to enter the kingdom of God.”<sup class="nt-fn">[[Luke 18 — OSB Notes#^f4643|†]]</sup> ^v25
