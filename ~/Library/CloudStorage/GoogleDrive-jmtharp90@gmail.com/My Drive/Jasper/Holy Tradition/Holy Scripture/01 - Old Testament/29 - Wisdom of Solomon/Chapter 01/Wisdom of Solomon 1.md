*Seek the Lord*
###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> Love righteousness, you who judge on the earth. Think about the Lord in goodness And seek Him with sincerity of heart;<sup class="nt-fn">[[Wisdom of Solomon 1 — OSB Notes#^f2655|†]]</sup> ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> Because He is found by those who do not tempt Him, And He is manifest to those who do not disbelieve Him. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> For dishonest reasoning separates people from God, And when His power examines someone, It convicts the undiscerning; ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> For wisdom will not enter the soul that plots evil, Nor will it dwell in a body involved in sin.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Wisdom of Solomon 1 — OSB Notes#^f2656|†]]</sup> ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> For a holy spirit of discipline flees from deceit And sends away undiscerning reasoning; It will put wrongdoing to shame when it comes near.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Wisdom of Solomon 1 — OSB Notes#^f2657|†]]</sup> ^v5

*God Examines Our Actions*
###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> For wisdom is a spirit that loves mankind, But she will punish a blasphemer because of his words; For God is the witness of his thoughts, The true examiner of his heart, and the hearer of his tongue; ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> Because the Spirit of the Lord fills the world, And He who holds all things together knows what is said.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Wisdom of Solomon 1 — OSB Notes#^f2658|†]]</sup> ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Therefore no one who speaks unrighteous things Will escape notice, Nor will justice, when it cross-examines, pass him by. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> For there shall be a close examination Into the deliberations of an ungodly man, And a report of his words will come to the Lord As proof of his lawlessness; ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> For the zealous ear hears all things, And the noise of murmuring is not hidden. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> Therefore keep yourself from useless murmuring And refrain your tongue from evil speech; For no secret word will go unpunished, And a lying mouth will destroy one's soul. ^v11

*God Made Life, Not Death*
###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> Do not be zealous for death by the deceit of your life, Nor bring destruction upon yourself by the works of your hands. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> For God did not make death, Neither does He have pleasure over the destruction of the living.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Wisdom of Solomon 1 — OSB Notes#^f2659|†]]</sup> ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> For He created all things that they might exist, And the generations of the world so they might be preserved; For there was no poison of death in them, Nor was the reign of Hades on the earth. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> For righteousness does not die. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> But the ungodly summoned death by their words and works; Although they thought death would be a friend, they were dissolved. For they made a covenant with death, Since they were deserving to share it in common. ^v16
