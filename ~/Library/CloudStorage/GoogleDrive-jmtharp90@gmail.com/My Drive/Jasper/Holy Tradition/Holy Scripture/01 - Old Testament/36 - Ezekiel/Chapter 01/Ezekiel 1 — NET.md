*A Vision of God’s Glory*
###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> In the thirtieth year, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n260011|†]]</sup> on the fifth day of the fourth month, while I was among the exiles <sup class="nt-fn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n260012|†]]</sup> at the Kebar River, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n260013|†]]</sup> the heavens opened <sup class="nt-fn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n260014|†]]</sup> and I saw a divine vision. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n260015|*]]</sup> ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> (On the fifth day of the month—it was the fifth year of King Jehoiachin’s exile— ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> the Lord’ s message came to the priest Ezekiel <sup class="nt-fn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n260016|†]]</sup> the son of Buzi, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n260017|*]]</sup> at the Kebar River in the land of the Babylonians. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n260018|*]]</sup> The hand <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n260019|*]]</sup> of the Lord came on him there.) ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> As I watched, I noticed <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600110|*]]</sup> a windstorm <sup class="nt-fn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600111|†]]</sup> coming from the north—an enormous cloud, with lightning flashing, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600112|*]]</sup> such that bright light <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600113|*]]</sup> rimmed it and came from <sup class="nt-tc">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600114|‡]]</sup> it like glowing amber <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600115|*]]</sup> from the middle of a fire. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> In the fire <sup class="nt-tc">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600116|‡]]</sup> were what looked like <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600117|*]]</sup> four living beings. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600118|*]]</sup> In their appearance they had human form, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600119|†]]</sup> ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> but each had four faces and four wings. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> Their legs were straight, but the soles of their feet were like calves’ feet. They gleamed <sup class="nt-fn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600120|†]]</sup> like polished bronze. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> They had human hands <sup class="nt-tc">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600121|‡]]</sup> under their wings on their four sides. As for the faces and wings of the four of them, ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> their wings touched each other; they did not turn as they moved, but went straight ahead. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600122|*]]</sup> ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> Their faces had this appearance: Each of the four had the face of a man, with the face of a lion on the right, the face of an ox on the left, and also the face of an eagle. <sup class="nt-tc">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600123|‡]]</sup> ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> Their wings were spread out above them; each had two wings touching the wings of one of the other beings on either side and two wings covering their bodies. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> Each moved straight ahead <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600124|*]]</sup> —wherever the spirit <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600125|*]]</sup> would go, they would go, without turning as they went. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> In the middle <sup class="nt-tc">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600126|‡]]</sup> of the living beings was something like <sup class="nt-tc">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600127|‡]]</sup> burning coals of fire <sup class="nt-fn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600128|†]]</sup> or like torches. It moved back and forth among the living beings. It was bright, and lightning was flashing out of the fire. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> The living beings moved backward and forward as quickly as flashes of lightning. <sup class="nt-tc">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600129|‡]]</sup> ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> Then I looked, <sup class="nt-tc">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600130|‡]]</sup> and I saw one wheel <sup class="nt-fn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600131|†]]</sup> on the ground <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600132|*]]</sup> beside each of the four beings. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> The appearance of the wheels and their construction <sup class="nt-tc">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600133|‡]]</sup> was like gleaming jasper, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600134|*]]</sup> and all four wheels looked alike. Their structure was like a wheel within a wheel. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600135|*]]</sup> ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> When they moved they would go in any of the four directions they faced without turning as they moved. ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> Their rims were high and awesome, <sup class="nt-tc">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600136|‡]]</sup> and the rims of all four wheels were full of eyes all around. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> When the living beings moved, the wheels beside them moved; when the living beings rose up from the ground, the wheels rose up too. ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> Wherever the spirit <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600137|*]]</sup> would go, they would go, <sup class="nt-tc">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600138|‡]]</sup> and the wheels would rise up beside them because the spirit <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600139|*]]</sup> of the living being was in the wheel. ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> When the living beings moved, the wheels moved, and when they stopped moving, the wheels stopped. <sup class="nt-tc">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600140|‡]]</sup> When they rose up from the ground, the wheels rose up from the ground; the wheels rose up beside them because the spirit of the living being was in the wheel. ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> Over the heads of the living beings was something like a platform, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600141|*]]</sup> glittering awesomely like ice, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600142|*]]</sup> stretched out over their heads. ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> Under the platform their wings were stretched out, each toward the other. Each of the beings also had two wings covering <sup class="nt-tc">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600143|‡]]</sup> its body. ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> When they moved, I heard the sound of their wings—it was like the sound of rushing waters, or the voice of the Sovereign One, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600144|*]]</sup> or the tumult <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600145|*]]</sup> of an army. When they stood still, they lowered their wings. ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> Then there was a voice from above the platform over their heads when they stood still. <sup class="nt-tc">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600146|‡]]</sup> ^v25

###### v26
<span class="vn">26</span> Above the platform over their heads was something like a sapphire shaped like a throne. High above on the throne was a form that appeared to be a man. ^v26

###### v27
<span class="vn">27</span> I saw an amber glow <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600147|*]]</sup> like a fire enclosed all around <sup class="nt-tc">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600148|‡]]</sup> from his waist up. From his waist down I saw something that looked like fire. There was a brilliant light around it, ^v27

###### v28
<span class="vn">28</span> like the appearance of a rainbow in the clouds after the rain. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600149|†]]</sup> This was the appearance of the surrounding brilliant light; it looked like the glory of the Lord. When I saw <sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — NET Notes#^n2600150|*]]</sup> it, I threw myself face down, and I heard a voice speaking. ^v28
