*The Beatitudes*
###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> When <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n400051|*]]</sup> he saw the crowds, he went up the mountain. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n400052|*]]</sup> After he sat down his disciples came to him. ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> Then <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n400053|*]]</sup> he began to teach <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n400054|*]]</sup> them by saying: ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> “Blessed <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n400055|†]]</sup> are the poor in spirit, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n400056|†]]</sup> for the kingdom of heaven belongs <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n400057|†]]</sup> to them. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> “Blessed are those who mourn, for they will be comforted. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n400058|†]]</sup> ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> “Blessed are the meek, for they will inherit the earth. ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> “Blessed are those who hunger <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n400059|†]]</sup> and thirst for righteousness, for they will be satisfied. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> “Blessed are the merciful, for they will be shown mercy. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> “Blessed are the pure in heart, for they will see God. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> “Blessed are the peacemakers, for they will be called the children <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000510|*]]</sup> of God. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> “Blessed are those who are persecuted for righteousness, for the kingdom of heaven belongs to them. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> “Blessed are you when people <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000511|*]]</sup> insult you and persecute you and say all kinds of evil things about you falsely <sup class="nt-tc">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000512|‡]]</sup> on account of me. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> Rejoice and be glad, because your reward is great in heaven, for they persecuted the prophets before you in the same way. ^v12

*Salt and Light*
###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> “You are the salt <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000513|†]]</sup> of the earth. But if salt loses its flavor, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000514|†]]</sup> how can it be made salty again? It is no longer good for anything except to be thrown out and trampled on by people! ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> You are the light of the world. A city located on a hill cannot be hidden. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> People <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000515|*]]</sup> do not light a lamp and put it under a basket <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000516|*]]</sup> but on a lampstand, and it gives light to all in the house. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> In the same way, let your light shine before people, so that they can see your good deeds and give honor to your Father in heaven. ^v16

*Fulfillment of the Law and Prophets*
###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> “Do not think that I have come to abolish the law or the prophets. I have not come to abolish these things but to fulfill them. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000517|*]]</sup> ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> I <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000518|*]]</sup> tell you the truth, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000519|*]]</sup> until heaven and earth pass away not the smallest letter or stroke of a letter <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000520|*]]</sup> will pass from the law until everything takes place. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> So anyone who breaks one of the least of these commands and teaches others <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000521|*]]</sup> to do so will be called least in the kingdom of heaven, but whoever obeys them and teaches others to do so will be called great in the kingdom of heaven. ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> For I tell you, unless your righteousness goes beyond that of the experts in the law <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000522|*]]</sup> and the Pharisees, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000523|†]]</sup> you will never enter the kingdom of heaven! ^v20

*Anger and Murder*
###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> “You have heard that it was said to an older generation, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000524|*]]</sup> ‘ Do not murder,’ <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000525|†]]</sup> and ‘whoever murders will be subjected to judgment.’ <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000526|†]]</sup> ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> But I say to you that anyone who is angry with a brother <sup class="nt-tc">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000527|‡]]</sup> will be subjected to judgment. And whoever insults <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000528|*]]</sup> a brother will be brought before <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000529|*]]</sup> the council, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000530|*]]</sup> and whoever says ‘Fool’ <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000531|*]]</sup> will be sent <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000532|*]]</sup> to fiery hell. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000533|*]]</sup> ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> So then, if you bring your gift to the altar and there you remember that your brother has something against you, ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> leave your gift there in front of the altar. First go and be reconciled to your brother and then come and present your gift. ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> Reach agreement <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000534|*]]</sup> quickly with your accuser while on the way to court, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000535|*]]</sup> or he <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000536|*]]</sup> may hand you over to the judge, and the judge hand you over to the warden, and you will be thrown into prison. ^v25

###### v26
<span class="vn">26</span> I tell you the truth, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000537|*]]</sup> you will never get out of there until you have paid the last penny! <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000538|*]]</sup> ^v26

*Adultery*
###### v27
<span class="vn">27</span> “You have heard that it was said, ‘ Do not commit adultery.’ <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000539|†]]</sup> ^v27

###### v28
<span class="vn">28</span> But I say to you that whoever looks at a woman to desire her has already committed adultery with her in his heart. ^v28

###### v29
<span class="vn">29</span> If your right eye causes you to sin, tear it out and throw it away! It is better to lose one of your members than to have your whole body thrown into hell. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000540|†]]</sup> ^v29

###### v30
<span class="vn">30</span> If your right hand causes you to sin, cut it off and throw it away! It is better to lose one of your members than to have your whole body go into hell. ^v30

*Divorce*
###### v31
<span class="vn">31</span> “It was said, ‘ Whoever divorces his wife must give her a legal document.’ <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000541|*]]</sup> ^v31

###### v32
<span class="vn">32</span> But I say to you that everyone who divorces his wife, except for immorality, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000542|†]]</sup> makes her commit adultery, and whoever marries a divorced woman commits adultery. ^v32

*Oaths*
###### v33
<span class="vn">33</span> “Again, you have heard that it was said to an older generation, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000543|*]]</sup> ‘ Do not break an oath, but fulfill your vows to the Lord.’ <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000544|†]]</sup> ^v33

###### v34
<span class="vn">34</span> But I say to you, do not take oaths at all—not by heaven, because it is the throne of God, ^v34

###### v35
<span class="vn">35</span> not by earth, because it is his footstool, and not by Jerusalem, because it is the city of the great King. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000546|†]]</sup> ^v35

###### v36
<span class="vn">36</span> Do not take an oath by your head, because you are not able to make one hair white or black. ^v36

###### v37
<span class="vn">37</span> Let your word be ‘Yes, yes’ or ‘No, no.’ More than this is from the evil one. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000547|*]]</sup> ^v37

*Retaliation*
###### v38
<span class="vn">38</span> “You have heard that it was said, ‘ An eye for an eye and a tooth for a tooth.’ <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000548|†]]</sup> ^v38

###### v39
<span class="vn">39</span> But I say to you, do not resist the evildoer. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000549|*]]</sup> But whoever strikes you on the <sup class="nt-tc">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000550|‡]]</sup> right cheek, turn the other to him as well. ^v39

###### v40
<span class="vn">40</span> And if someone wants to sue you and take your tunic, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000551|*]]</sup> let him have your coat also. ^v40

###### v41
<span class="vn">41</span> And if anyone forces you to go one mile, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000552|†]]</sup> go with him two. ^v41

###### v42
<span class="vn">42</span> Give to the one who asks you, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000553|†]]</sup> and do not reject <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000554|*]]</sup> the one who wants to borrow from you. ^v42

*Love for Enemies*
###### v43
<span class="vn">43</span> “You have heard that it was said, ‘ Love your neighbor ’ <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000555|†]]</sup> and ‘hate your enemy.’ <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000556|†]]</sup> ^v43

###### v44
<span class="vn">44</span> But I say to you, love your enemy and <sup class="nt-tc">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000557|‡]]</sup> pray for those who persecute you, ^v44

###### v45
<span class="vn">45</span> so that you may be like <sup class="nt-tn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000558|*]]</sup> your Father in heaven, since he causes the sun to rise on the evil and the good, and sends rain on the righteous and the unrighteous. ^v45

###### v46
<span class="vn">46</span> For if you love those who love you, what reward do you have? Even the tax collectors <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000559|†]]</sup> do the same, don’t they? ^v46

###### v47
<span class="vn">47</span> And if you only greet your brothers, what more do you do? Even the Gentiles do the same, don’t they? ^v47

###### v48
<span class="vn">48</span> So then, be perfect, as your heavenly Father is perfect. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — NET Notes#^n4000560|†]]</sup> ^v48
