*The Creation of the World*
###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> In the beginning <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n010011|*]]</sup> God <sup class="nt-fn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n010012|†]]</sup> created <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n010013|*]]</sup> the heavens and the earth. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n010014|*]]</sup> ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> Now <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n010015|*]]</sup> the earth <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n010016|*]]</sup> was without shape and empty, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n010017|*]]</sup> and darkness <sup class="nt-fn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n010018|†]]</sup> was over the surface of the watery deep, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n010019|*]]</sup> but the Spirit of God <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100110|*]]</sup> was moving <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100111|*]]</sup> over the surface <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100112|*]]</sup> of the water. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100113|†]]</sup> ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> God said, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100114|*]]</sup> “Let there be <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100115|*]]</sup> light.” <sup class="nt-fn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100116|†]]</sup> And there was light! ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> God saw <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100117|*]]</sup> that the light was good, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100118|*]]</sup> so God separated <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100119|*]]</sup> the light from the darkness. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> God called <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100120|*]]</sup> the light “day” and the darkness <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100121|*]]</sup> “night.” There was evening, and there was morning, marking the first day. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100122|*]]</sup> ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> God said, “Let there be an expanse <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100123|*]]</sup> in the midst of the waters and let it separate water <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100124|*]]</sup> from water.” ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> So God made the expanse and separated the water under the expanse from the water above it. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100125|*]]</sup> It was so. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100126|*]]</sup> ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> God called the expanse “sky.” <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100127|*]]</sup> There was evening, and there was morning, a second day. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> God said, “Let the water under the sky be gathered to one place <sup class="nt-fn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100128|†]]</sup> and let dry ground appear.” <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100129|*]]</sup> It was so. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> God called the dry ground “land” <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100130|*]]</sup> and the gathered waters he called “seas.” God saw that it was good. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> God said, “Let the land produce vegetation: <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100131|*]]</sup> plants yielding seeds and <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100132|*]]</sup> trees on the land bearing fruit with seed in it, according to their kinds.” <sup class="nt-fn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100133|†]]</sup> It was so. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> The land produced vegetation—plants yielding seeds according to their kinds, and trees bearing fruit with seed in it according to their kinds. God saw that it was good. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> There was evening, and there was morning, a third day. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> God said, “Let there be lights <sup class="nt-fn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100134|†]]</sup> in the expanse <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100135|*]]</sup> of the sky to separate the day from the night, and let them be signs <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100136|*]]</sup> to indicate seasons and days and years, ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> and let them serve as lights in the expanse of the sky to give light on the earth.” It was so. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> God made two great lights <sup class="nt-fn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100137|†]]</sup> —the greater light to rule over the day and the lesser light to rule over the night. He made the stars also. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100138|*]]</sup> ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> God placed the lights <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100139|*]]</sup> in the expanse of the sky to shine on the earth, ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> to preside over the day and the night, and to separate the light from the darkness. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100140|†]]</sup> God saw that it was good. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> There was evening, and there was morning, a fourth day. ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> God said, “Let the water swarm with swarms <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100141|*]]</sup> of living creatures and let birds fly <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100142|*]]</sup> above the earth across the expanse of the sky.” ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> God created the great sea creatures <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100143|*]]</sup> and every living and moving thing with which the water swarmed, according to their kinds, and every winged bird according to its kind. God saw that it was good. ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> God blessed them <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100144|*]]</sup> and said, “Be fruitful and multiply and fill the water in the seas, and let the birds multiply on the earth.” <sup class="nt-fn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100145|†]]</sup> ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> There was evening, and there was morning, a fifth day. ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> God said, “Let the land produce living creatures according to their kinds: cattle, creeping things, and wild animals, each according to its kind.” <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100146|*]]</sup> It was so. ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> God made the wild animals according to their kinds, the cattle according to their kinds, and all the creatures that creep along the ground according to their kinds. God saw that it was good. ^v25

###### v26
<span class="vn">26</span> Then God said, “Let us make <sup class="nt-fn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100147|†]]</sup> humankind <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100148|*]]</sup> in our image, after our likeness, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100149|*]]</sup> so they may rule <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100150|*]]</sup> over the fish of the sea and the birds of the air, over the cattle, and over all the earth, <sup class="nt-tc">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100151|‡]]</sup> and over all the creatures that move <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100152|*]]</sup> on the earth.” ^v26

###### v27
<span class="vn">27</span> God created humankind <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100153|*]]</sup> in his own image, in the image of God he created them, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100154|*]]</sup> male and female he created them. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100155|†]]</sup> ^v27

###### v28
<span class="vn">28</span> God blessed <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100156|*]]</sup> them and said <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100157|*]]</sup> to them, “Be fruitful and multiply! Fill the earth and subdue it! <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100158|*]]</sup> Rule over the fish of the sea and the birds of the air and every creature that moves on the ground.” <sup class="nt-fn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100159|†]]</sup> ^v28

###### v29
<span class="vn">29</span> Then God said, “I now <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100160|*]]</sup> give you every seed-bearing plant on the face of the entire earth and every tree that has fruit with seed in it. They will be yours for food. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100161|†]]</sup> ^v29

###### v30
<span class="vn">30</span> And to all the animals of the earth, and to every bird of the air, and to all the creatures that move on the ground—everything that has living breath <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100162|*]]</sup> in it—I give <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100163|*]]</sup> every green plant for food.” It was so. ^v30

###### v31
<span class="vn">31</span> God saw all that he had made—and it was very good! <sup class="nt-tn">[[Genesis 1 — NET Notes#^n0100164|*]]</sup> There was evening, and there was morning, the sixth day. ^v31
