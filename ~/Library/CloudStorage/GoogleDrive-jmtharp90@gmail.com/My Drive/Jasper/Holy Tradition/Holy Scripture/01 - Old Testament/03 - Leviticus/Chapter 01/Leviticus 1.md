*Burnt Offerings*
###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> Now the Lord called to Moses, and spoke to him from the tabernacle of testimony, saying, ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> “Speak to the children of Israel, and say to them, ‘If a man among you should offer gifts to the Lord from the cattle—the oxen or the sheep—you shall offer your gifts.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Leviticus 1 — OSB Notes#^f414|†]]</sup> ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> If his gift should be a whole burnt offering from the oxen, he shall offer a male without blemish. He shall offer it at the door of the tabernacle of testimony as acceptable before the Lord.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Leviticus 1 — OSB Notes#^f415|†]]</sup> ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> Then he shall put his hand on the head of the burnt offering, and it will be accepted on his behalf to make atonement for him.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Leviticus 1 — OSB Notes#^f416|†]]</sup> ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> He shall kill the young bull before the Lord; and the priests, Aaron's sons, shall offer the blood and sprinkle the blood all around on the altar by the doors of the tabernacle of testimony.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Leviticus 1 — OSB Notes#^f417|†]]</sup> ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> Then he shall skin the whole burnt offering and cut it into its pieces. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> The sons of Aaron the priest shall put fire on the altar, and lay the wood on the fire. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Then the priests, Aaron's sons, shall lay the parts, the head and the fat, upon the wood and fire on the altar; ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> but he shall wash its entrails and its legs with water. The priest shall then put all of it on the altar as a burnt offering, a sacrifice for a sweet aroma to the Lord.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Leviticus 1 — OSB Notes#^f418|†]]</sup> ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> ‘If his gift to the Lord should be from the sheep—from the lambs or the kids—as a whole burnt offering, he shall offer a male without blemish, and put his hand on its head. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> He shall kill it on the north side of the altar before the Lord; and the priests, Aaron's sons, shall sprinkle its blood all around on the altar. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> Then they shall cut it into its pieces, with its head and its fat; and the priests shall lay them upon the wood and fire on the altar; ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> but he shall wash the entrails and the legs with water. Then the priest shall offer all of it, and put it on the altar as a burnt offering, a sacrifice for a sweet aroma to the Lord. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> ‘But if you should offer a gift to the Lord from the birds for a burnt offering, he shall offer his gift from the turtledoves or young pigeons. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> The priest shall offer it on the altar, wring off its head, and put it on the altar; its blood shall be drained out at the base of the altar. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> Then he shall remove its crop with its feathers, and cast it beside the altar on the east side, into the place for ashes. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> Then he shall split it at its wings, but shall not divide it; and the priest shall put it upon the wood and fire on the altar as a burnt offering, a sacrifice for a sweet aroma to the Lord. ^v17
