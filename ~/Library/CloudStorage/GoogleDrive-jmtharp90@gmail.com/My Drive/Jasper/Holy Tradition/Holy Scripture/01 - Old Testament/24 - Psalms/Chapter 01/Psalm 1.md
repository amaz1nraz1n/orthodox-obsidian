###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> Blessed is the man Who walks not in the counsel of the ungodly, Nor stands in the way of sinners, Nor sits in the seat of the troublesome;<sup class="nt-fn">[[Psalms 1 — OSB Notes#^f1997|†]]</sup> ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> But his will is in the law of the Lord, And in His law he meditates day and night. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> He shall be like a tree Planted by streams of waters, That produces its fruit in its season; And his leaf shall not wither, And whatever he does shall prosper. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> Not so are the ungodly, not so, But they are like the dust the wind drives from the face of the earth. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> Therefore the ungodly shall not rise in the judgment, Nor sinners in the counsel of the righteous; ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> For the Lord knows the way of the righteous, But the way of the ungodly shall perish. ^v6
