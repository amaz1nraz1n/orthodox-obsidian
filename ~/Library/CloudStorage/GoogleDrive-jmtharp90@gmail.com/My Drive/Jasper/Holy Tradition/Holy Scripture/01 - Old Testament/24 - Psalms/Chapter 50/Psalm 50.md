###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> For the End; a psalm by David;<sup class="nt-fn">[[Psalms 50 — OSB Notes#^f2046|†]]</sup><sup class="nt-lit">[[Psalms 50 — OSB Notes#^fx95|☩]]</sup> ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> when Nathan the prophet came to him, at the time he went in to Bathsheba. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> Have mercy on me, O God, according to Your great mercy; And according to the abundance of Your compassion, blot out my transgression. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> Wash me thoroughly from my lawlessness And cleanse me from my sin. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> For I know my lawlessness, And my sin is always before me. ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> Against You only have I sinned And done evil in Your sight; That You may be justified in Your words, And overcome when You are judged. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> For behold, I was conceived in transgressions, And in sins my mother bore me. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Behold, You love truth; You showed me the unknown and secret things of Your wisdom. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> You shall sprinkle me with hyssop, and I will be cleansed; You shall wash me, and I will be made whiter than snow. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> You shall make me hear joy and gladness; My bones that were humbled shall greatly rejoice. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> Turn Your face from my sins, And blot out all my transgressions. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> Create in me a clean heart, O God, And renew a right spirit within me. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> Do not cast me away from Your presence, And do not take Your Holy Spirit from me. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> Restore to me the joy of Your salvation, And uphold me with Your guiding Spirit. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> I will teach transgressors Your ways, And the ungodly shall turn back to You. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> Deliver me from bloodguiltiness, O God, the God of my salvation, And my tongue shall greatly rejoice in Your righteousness. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> O Lord, You shall open my lips, And my mouth will declare Your praise. ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> For if You desired sacrifice, I would give it; You will not be pleased with whole burnt offerings. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> A sacrifice to God is a broken spirit, A broken and humbled heart God will not despise. ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> Do good, O Lord, in Your good pleasure to Zion, And let the walls of Jerusalem be built; ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> Then You will be pleased with a sacrifice of righteousness, With offerings and whole burnt offerings; Then shall they offer young bulls on Your altar. ^v21
