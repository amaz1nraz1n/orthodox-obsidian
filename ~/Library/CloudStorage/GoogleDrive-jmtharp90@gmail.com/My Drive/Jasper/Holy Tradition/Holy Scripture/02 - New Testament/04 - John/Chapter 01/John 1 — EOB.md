###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> This one was in the beginning with God. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> All things came into being through him, and without him, nothing came into being that has come into being. <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1005|†]]</sup> ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> In him was life, life that was the light of mankind. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> The light shines in the darkness, and the darkness has not overcome <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1006|†]]</sup> it. ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> There came a man, sent from God, whose name was John. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> John came as a witness, to bear witness to the light, so that all might believe through him. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> He was not the light, but [he was sent] to bear witness to the light. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> The true light which enlightens everyone was coming into the world. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> He was in the world, and the world had come into existence through him, and the world did not recognize <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1007|†]]</sup> him. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> He came to his own [people], and those who were his own did not receive <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1008|†]]</sup> him. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> But as many as received him, to them he gave the ability <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1009|†]]</sup> to become God’s children, to those who believe in his Name. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> They <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1010|†]]</sup> were born not of blood, nor of the will of the flesh, nor of the will of man, but of God. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> The Word became flesh and made his dwelling <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1011|†]]</sup> among us. We beheld his glory, glory as a Father’s uniquely-begotten <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1012|†]]</sup> son, full of grace and truth. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> John testified about him; he cried out, saying, “This was the one of whom I said, ‘He who comes after me ranks ahead of me because he was before me.’” ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> And from his fullness, <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1013|†]]</sup> we have all received grace upon grace. <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1014|†]]</sup> ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> For the law was given through Moses, [but] grace and truth came through Jesus Christ. ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> No one has seen God at any time. The uniquely-begotten <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1015|†]]</sup> Son <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1016|†]]</sup> who is in the bosom of the Father, <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1017|†]]</sup> he has explained him. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> This is John’s testimony, when the Jews sent priests and Levites from Jerusalem to ask him, “Who are you?” ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> He admitted and did not deny [it], [plainly] admitting, “I am not the Christ!” ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> They asked him, “What then? Are you Elias (Elijah)?” He said, “I am not!” “Are you the prophet?” He answered, “No.” ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> Therefore, they asked him, “Who are you? Give us an answer to take back to those who sent us. What do you say about yourself?” ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> John said, “I am the voice of one crying in the wilderness, <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1018|†]]</sup> ‘Make straight the way of the Lord,’ <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1019|†]]</sup> as Isaias (Isaiah) the prophet said.” ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> Those who had been sent were from the Pharisees. ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> They asked him, “Why then do you baptize, if you are not the Christ, nor Elias (Elijah), nor the prophet?” ^v25

###### v26
<span class="vn">26</span> John answered them, “I baptize in water, but among you stands one whom you do not know. <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1020|†]]</sup> ^v26

###### v27
<span class="vn">27</span> He is the one who comes after me, who is ranked before me, <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1021|†]]</sup> whose sandal strap I am not worthy to untie.” ^v27

###### v28
<span class="vn">28</span> These things took place in Bethany beyond the Jordan, where John was baptizing. ^v28

###### v29
<span class="vn">29</span> The next day, John <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1022|†]]</sup> saw Jesus coming to him and said, “Behold, the Lamb of God who takes away the sin of the world! ^v29

###### v30
<span class="vn">30</span> This is the one of whom I said, ‘After me comes a man who is ranked before me, because he was before me.’ ^v30

###### v31
<span class="vn">31</span> I did not know him, but for this reason I came baptizing in water: so that he would be revealed to Israel.” ^v31

###### v32
<span class="vn">32</span> John bore witness, saying, “I saw the Spirit descending as a dove out of heaven and remain on him. ^v32

###### v33
<span class="vn">33</span> I did not know him, but the one who sent me to baptize in water said to me, ‘Upon whom you will see the Spirit descending and remaining, this is he who baptizes in the Holy Spirit.’ ^v33

###### v34
<span class="vn">34</span> Moreover, I myself have seen and testified that this is the Son of God.” <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1023|†]]</sup> ^v34

###### v35
<span class="vn">35</span> Again, the next day, John was standing with two of his disciples. ^v35

###### v36
<span class="vn">36</span> He looked at Jesus as he was walking by and said, “Behold, the Lamb of God!” ^v36

###### v37
<span class="vn">37</span> The two disciples heard him speak, and they began to follow Jesus. ^v37

###### v38
<span class="vn">38</span> Turning and seeing them following, Jesus asked them, “What are you looking for?” They said to him, “Rabbi” (which translated means Teacher), “where are you staying?” ^v38

###### v39
<span class="vn">39</span> Jesus <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1024|†]]</sup> said to them, “Come and see!” They came therefore and saw where he was staying, and they remained with him that day. (It was about the tenth hour). <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1025|†]]</sup> ^v39

###### v40
<span class="vn">40</span> One of the two who heard John and who began to follow Jesus was Andrew, Simon Peter’s brother. ^v40

###### v41
<span class="vn">41</span> He first found his own brother, Simon and said to him, “We have found the Messiah!” <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1026|†]]</sup> (which translated means Christ). <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1027|†]]</sup> ^v41

###### v42
<span class="vn">42</span> And Andrew <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1028|†]]</sup> brought him to Jesus who looked at him, and said, “You are Simon the son of Jonah. <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1029|†]]</sup> You shall be called Kephas” (which translated means Peter). ^v42

###### v43
<span class="vn">43</span> On the next day, being determined to go out into Galilee, Jesus <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1030|†]]</sup> met Philip and said to him, “Follow me!” ^v43

###### v44
<span class="vn">44</span> Now Philip was from Bethsaida, the city of Andrew and Peter. ^v44

###### v45
<span class="vn">45</span> Philip found Nathaniel and said to him, “We have found the one of whom Moses in the law and the prophets, wrote: Jesus of Nazareth, the son of Joseph!” ^v45

###### v46
<span class="vn">46</span> Nathanael asked him, “Can anything good come out of Nazareth?” Philip replied, “Come and see!” ^v46

###### v47
<span class="vn">47</span> As Jesus saw Nathaniel coming to him, he said about him, “Behold, an Israelite indeed, in whom there is no deceit!” ^v47

###### v48
<span class="vn">48</span> Nathanael asked Jesus, “How do you know me?” Jesus answered and said to him, “Before Philip called you, when you were under the fig tree, I saw you.” ^v48

###### v49
<span class="vn">49</span> Nathanael replied, “Rabbi, you are the Son of God! You are King of Israel!” ^v49

###### v50
<span class="vn">50</span> Jesus said to him, “Because I told you, ‘I saw you underneath the fig tree,’ do you believe? You will see greater things than these!” ^v50

###### v51
<span class="vn">51</span> He said to Philip, “Amen, amen, I tell you; hereafter, you <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1031|†]]</sup> will see heaven opened, and the angels of God ascending and descending upon <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 1 — EOB Notes#^edn1032|†]]</sup> the Son of Man.” ^v51
