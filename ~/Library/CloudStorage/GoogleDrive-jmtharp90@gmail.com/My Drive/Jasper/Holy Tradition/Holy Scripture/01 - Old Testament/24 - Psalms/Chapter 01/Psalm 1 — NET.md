<span class="vn">1</span> How blessed <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 1 — NET Notes#^n190012|*]]</sup> is the one <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 1 — NET Notes#^n190013|*]]</sup> who does not follow <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 1 — NET Notes#^n190014|*]]</sup> the advice <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 1 — NET Notes#^n190015|*]]</sup> of the wicked, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 1 — NET Notes#^n190016|*]]</sup> or stand in the pathway <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 1 — NET Notes#^n190017|*]]</sup> with sinners, or sit in the assembly <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 1 — NET Notes#^n190018|*]]</sup> of scoffers. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 1 — NET Notes#^n190019|*]]</sup> ^v1
