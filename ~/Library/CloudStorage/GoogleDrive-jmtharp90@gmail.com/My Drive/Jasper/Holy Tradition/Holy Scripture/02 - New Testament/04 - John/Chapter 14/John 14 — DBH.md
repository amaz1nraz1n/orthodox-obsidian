> **Nav:** [[John 14|Hub]] · [[John 14 — DBH Notes|DBH Notes]] · [[John 14 — NET Notes|NET Notes]]

###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> “Do not let your heart be troubled; have faith in God and have faith in me. ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> In my Father’s house there are many places of rest. Would I otherwise have told you that I am going to make a place ready for you? ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> And if I go and make a place ready for you, I am coming again and will take you along with me, so that where I am you might be also. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> And you know the way to where I am going.” ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> Thomas says to him, “Lord, we do not know where you are going. How do we know the way?” ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> Jesus says to him, “I am the way and the truth and the life; no one comes to the Father except through me. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> If you had known me you would also have recognized my Father. From this moment you know and have seen him.” ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Philip says to him, “Lord, show us the Father, and for us that suffices.” ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> Jesus says to him, “I am with you for such a long time, Philip, and you have not known me? Whoever has seen me has seen the Father. How can you say, ‘Show us the Father’? ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> Do you not believe that I am in the Father and that the Father is in me? The words that I utter to you I do not speak from myself; but the Father, abiding in me, performs his works. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> Have faith in me, that I am in the Father and that the Father is in me; or else have faith on account of the works themselves. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> Amen, amen, I tell you, whoever has faith in me, the works I perform he will perform also, because I am going to the Father. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> And whatever you request in my name, this I will do, so that the Father might be glorified in the Son. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> If you ask anything of me in my name, I will do it. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> If you love me, you will keep my commandments. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> And I shall entreat the Father, and he will give you another Advocate, that he may be with you throughout the age,<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — DBH Notes#^fn313|†]]</sup> ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> The Spirit of truth, which the cosmos cannot receive because it neither sees nor knows it; you know it because it abides with you and will be within you. ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> I shall not leave you orphans; I am coming to you. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> Just a little while and the cosmos no longer sees me, but you see me; because I live, you too will live. ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> On that day you will know that I am in my Father, and you are in me, and I am in you. ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> Whoever has my commandments and keeps them, that one is the one who loves me, and whoever loves me will be loved by my Father, and I will love him and will manifest myself to him.” ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> Judas—not Iscariot—says to him, “Lord, what has happened then, that you are about to manifest yourself to us and not to the cosmos?” ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> Jesus answered and said to him, “If someone loves me, he will keep my word, and my Father will love him, and we will come to him and will make our home with him. ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> Whoever does not love me does not keep my words; and the word that you hear is not mine but rather that of the Father who has sent me. ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> These things I have spoken to you while remaining with you; ^v25

###### v26
<span class="vn">26</span> But the advocate, the Spirit, the Holy One, which the Father will send in my name, he will teach you everything and will remind you of everything I have told you. ^v26

###### v27
<span class="vn">27</span> Peace I leave you, my peace I give to you; I give to you not as the cosmos gives. Do not let your heart be troubled, neither let it be afraid. ^v27

###### v28
<span class="vn">28</span> You heard that I told you that I am going away and am coming to you. If you had loved me you would have rejoiced that I am going to the Father, because the Father is greater than I. ^v28

###### v29
<span class="vn">29</span> And now I have told you before it happens, so that when it happens you might have faith. ^v29

###### v30
<span class="vn">30</span> I will no longer speak much with you, for the Archon of the cosmos is coming—and he has no hold in me— ^v30

###### v31
<span class="vn">31</span> But so that the cosmos may know that I love the Father, and that just as the Father has commanded me so I do. Arise, let us go from here. ^v31
