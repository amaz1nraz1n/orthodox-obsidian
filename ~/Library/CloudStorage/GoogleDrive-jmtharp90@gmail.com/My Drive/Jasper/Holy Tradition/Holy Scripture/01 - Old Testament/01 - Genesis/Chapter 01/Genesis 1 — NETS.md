###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> In the beginning God made the sky and the earth. ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> Yet the earth was invisible and unformed, and darkness was over the abyss, and a divine wind was being carried along over the water. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> And God said, “Let light come into being.” And light came into being. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> And God saw the light, that it was good. And God separated between the light and between the darkness. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> And God called the light Day and the darkness he called Night. And it came to be evening, and it came to be morning, day one. ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> And God said, “Let a firmament come into being in the midst of the water, and let it be a separator between water and water.” And it became so. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> And God made the firmament, and God separated between the water that was under the firmament and between the water that was above the firmament. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> And God called the firmament Sky. And God saw that it was good. And it came to be evening, and it came to be morning, a second day. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> And God said, “Let the water that is under the sky be gathered into one gathering, and let the dry land appear.” And it became so. And the water that was under the sky was gathered into their gatherings, and the dry land appeared. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> And God called the dry land Earth, and the systems of the waters he called Seas. And God saw that it was good. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> And God said, “Let the earth put forth herbaceous vegetation, seeding seed according to kind and according to likeness, and a fruit-bearing tree producing fruit of which its seed is in it according to kind, on the earth.” And it became so. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> And the earth brought forth herbaceous vegetation, seeding seed according to kind and according to likeness, and a fruit-bearing tree producing fruit of which its seed is in it according to kind, on the earth. And God saw that it was good. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> And it came to be evening, and it came to be morning, a third day. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> And God said, “Let luminaries come into being in the firmament of the sky for illumination of the earth, to separate between the day and between the night, and let them be for signs and for seasons and for days and for years, ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> and let them be for illumination in the firmament of the sky so as to give light upon the earth.” And it became so. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> And God made the two great luminaries, the great luminary for rulership of the day and the lesser luminary for rulership of the night, and the stars. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> And God set them in the firmament of the sky so as to give light upon the earth ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> and to rule the day and the night and to separate between the light and between the darkness. And God saw that it was good. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> And it came to be evening, and it came to be morning, a fourth day. ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> And God said, “Let the waters bring forth creeping things among living creatures and birds flying on the earth against the firmament of the sky.” And it became so. ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> And God made the great sea monsters and every creature among creeping animals, which the waters brought forth according to their kinds, and every winged bird according to kind. And God saw that they were good. ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> And God blessed them, saying, “Increase, and multiply, and fill the waters in the seas, and let birds multiply on the earth.” ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> And it came to be evening, and it came to be morning, a fifth day. ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> And God said, “Let the earth bring forth the living creature according to kind: quadrupeds and creeping things and wild animals of the earth according to kind.” And it became so. ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> And God made the wild animals of the earth according to kind and the cattle according to kind and all the creeping things of the earth according to their kind. And God saw that they were good. ^v25

###### v26
<span class="vn">26</span> Then God said, “Let us make humankind according to our image and according to likeness, and let them rule the fish of the sea and the birds of the sky and the cattle and all the earth and all the creeping things that creep upon the earth.” ^v26

###### v27
<span class="vn">27</span> And God made humankind; according to divine image he made it; male and female he made them. ^v27

###### v28
<span class="vn">28</span> And God blessed them, saying, “Increase, and multiply, and fill the earth, and subdue it, and rule the fish of the sea and the birds of the sky and all the cattle and all the earth and all the creeping things that creep upon the earth.” ^v28

###### v29
<span class="vn">29</span> And God said, “See, I have given to you any herbage, sowable, seeding seed, which is over all the earth, and any tree that has in itself fruit of sowable seed—to you it shall be for food— ^v29

###### v30
<span class="vn">30</span> and to all the wild animals of the earth and to all the birds of the sky and to every creeping thing that creeps on the earth that has in itself the animating force of life—and all green herbage for food.” And it became so. ^v30

###### v31
<span class="vn">31</span> And God saw all the things that he had made, and see, they were exceedingly good. And it came to be evening, and it came to be morning, a sixth day. ^v31
