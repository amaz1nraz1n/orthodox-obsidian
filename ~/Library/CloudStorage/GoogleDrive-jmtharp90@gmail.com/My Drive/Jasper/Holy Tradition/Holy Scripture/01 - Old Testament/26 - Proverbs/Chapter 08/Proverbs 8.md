*Divine Wisdom for Mankind*
###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> You shall proclaim wisdom That discernment may obey you;<sup class="nt-fn">[[Proverbs 8 — OSB Notes#^f2307|†]]</sup><sup class="nt-fn">[[Proverbs 8 — OSB Notes#^f2308|†]]</sup><sup class="nt-lit">[[Proverbs 8 — OSB Notes#^fx169|☩]]</sup> ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> For she is on the lofty summit And stands in the midst of the paths.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Proverbs 8 — OSB Notes#^f2309|†]]</sup> ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> For she sits beside the gates of princes And sings at the entrances, ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> “You, O men, I exhort, And I utter my voice to the sons of men;<sup class="nt-fn">[[Proverbs 8 — OSB Notes#^f2310|†]]</sup> ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> Understand astuteness, O simple ones, And put it in your heart, O uninstructed ones. ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> Obey me, for I speak sacred things, And from my lips I will bring forth things that are true. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> For my mouth shall speak the truth, But false lips are an abomination before me.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Proverbs 8 — OSB Notes#^f2311|†]]</sup> ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> All the words of my mouth are with righteousness; There is nothing in them crooked or twisted. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> All things are evident to those who understand And true to those who find knowledge.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Proverbs 8 — OSB Notes#^f2312|†]]</sup> ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> Lay hold of instruction and not silver, And knowledge rather than tested gold, And choose perception rather than pure gold.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Proverbs 8 — OSB Notes#^f2313|†]]</sup> ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> For wisdom is better than precious stones, And every costly thing is not worthy of her. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> I, wisdom, dwell with counsel, And I call upon knowledge and understanding. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> The fear of the Lord hates unrighteousness, And both rudeness and arrogance, and the ways of wicked men; And I hate the perverted ways of evil men. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> Counsel and safety are mine; Discernment is mine, and strength is mine; ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> By me, kings reign, And princes write of righteousness; ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> By me, noblemen become great, And by me, monarchs have power in the land. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> I love those who love me, And those who seek me shall find me. ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> Riches and glory belong to me, And abundant possessions, and righteousness; ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> It is better to enjoy my fruits than gold and precious stones, And my fruits are better than choice silver. ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> I walk in the ways of righteousness, And I am conversant with the paths of judgment; ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> That I may distribute substance to those who love me, And may fill their treasuries with good things. ^v21

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> Before He made the earth, and before He made the abysses, Before the going forth of the fountains of waters,<sup class="nt-fn">[[Proverbs 8 — OSB Notes#^f2316|†]]</sup> ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> Before the mountains were created; And He begot me before all hills. ^v25

###### v30

###### v31

###### v32
<span class="vn">32</span> Now therefore, my son, hear me: Blessed is the man who shall hearken to me And the man who shall guard my ways;<sup class="nt-fn">[[Proverbs 8 — OSB Notes#^f2320|†]]</sup><sup class="nt-lit">[[Proverbs 8 — OSB Notes#^fx171|☩]]</sup> ^v32

###### v34
<span class="vn">34</span> For my issues are the issues of life, And grace is prepared from the Lord.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Proverbs 8 — OSB Notes#^f2321|†]]</sup> ^v34
