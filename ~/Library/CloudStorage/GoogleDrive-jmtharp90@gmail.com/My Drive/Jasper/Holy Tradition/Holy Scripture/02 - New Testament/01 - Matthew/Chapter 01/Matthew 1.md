*The Genealogy of Jesus*
###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> The book of the genealogy of Jesus Christ, the Son of David, the Son of Abraham:<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — OSB Notes#^f4077|†]]</sup><sup class="nt-lit">[[Matthew 1 — OSB Notes#^fx221|☩]]</sup> ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> Abraham begot Isaac, Isaac begot Jacob, and Jacob begot Judah and his brothers.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — OSB Notes#^f4078|†]]</sup> ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> Judah begot Perez and Zerah by Tamar, Perez begot Hezron, and Hezron begot Ram.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — OSB Notes#^f4079|†]]</sup> ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> Ram begot Amminadab, Amminadab begot Nahshon, and Nahshon begot Salmon. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> Salmon begot Boaz by Rahab, Boaz begot Obed by Ruth, Obed begot Jesse, ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> and Jesse begot David the king. David the king begot Solomon by her who had been the wife<sup class="nt-cross">[[Matthew 1 — OSB Notes#^fcross1|§]]</sup> of Uriah.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — OSB Notes#^f4080|†]]</sup> ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> Solomon begot Rehoboam, Rehoboam begot Abijah, and Abijah begot Asa.<sup class="nt-tc">[[Matthew 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar1|‡]]</sup> ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Asa begot Jehoshaphat, Jehoshaphat begot Joram, and Joram begot Uzziah. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> Uzziah begot Jotham, Jotham begot Ahaz, and Ahaz begot Hezekiah. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> Hezekiah begot Manasseh, Manasseh begot Amon,<sup class="nt-tc">[[Matthew 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar2|‡]]</sup> and Amon begot Josiah. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> Josiah begot Jeconiah and his brothers about the time they were carried away to Babylon. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> And after they were brought to Babylon, Jeconiah begot Shealtiel, and Shealtiel begot Zerubbabel. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> Zerubbabel begot Abiud, Abiud begot Eliakim, and Eliakim begot Azor. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> Azor begot Zadok, Zadok begot Achim, and Achim begot Eliud. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> Eliud begot Eleazar, Eleazar begot Matthan, and Matthan begot Jacob. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> And Jacob begot Joseph the husband of Mary, of whom was born Jesus who is called Christ.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — OSB Notes#^f4081|†]]</sup> ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> So all the generations from Abraham to David are fourteen generations, from David until the captivity in Babylon are fourteen generations, and from the captivity in Babylon until the Christ are fourteen generations.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — OSB Notes#^f4082|†]]</sup> ^v17

*The Virgin Birth of Christ*
###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> Now the birth of Jesus Christ was as follows: After His mother Mary was betrothed to Joseph, before they came together, she was found with child of the Holy Spirit. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> Then Joseph her husband, being a just man, and not wanting to make her a public example, was minded to put her away secretly.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — OSB Notes#^f4083|†]]</sup> ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> But while he thought about these things, behold, an angel of the Lord appeared to him in a dream, saying, “Joseph, son of David, do not be afraid to take to you Mary your wife, for that which is conceived in her is of the Holy Spirit.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — OSB Notes#^f4084|†]]</sup> ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> And she will bring forth a Son, and you shall call His name J ESUS , for He will save His people from their sins.” ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> So all this was done that it might be fulfilled which was spoken by the Lord through the prophet, saying:<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — OSB Notes#^f4085|†]]</sup> ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> “Behold, the virgin shall be with child, and bear a Son, and they shall call His name Immanuel,”<sup class="nt-cross">[[Matthew 1 — OSB Notes#^fcross2|§]]</sup> which is translated, “God with us.”<sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — OSB Notes#^f4086|†]]</sup> ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> Then Joseph, being aroused from sleep, did as the angel of the Lord commanded him and took to him his wife, ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> and did not know her till she had brought forth her firstborn Son.<sup class="nt-tc">[[Matthew 1 — OSB Notes#^fvar3|‡]]</sup> And he called His name J ESUS .<sup class="nt-cit">[[Matthew 1 — OSB Notes#^fcit35|¶]]</sup> ^v25
