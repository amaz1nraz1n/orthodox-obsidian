*The Superscription*
###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> The following is a record of what Jeremiah son of Hilkiah prophesied. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n240011|*]]</sup> He was one of the priests who lived at Anathoth in the territory of the tribe of Benjamin. ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> The Lord’ s <sup class="nt-fn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n240012|†]]</sup> message came to him <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n240013|*]]</sup> in the thirteenth year that Josiah son of Amon ruled over Judah. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> It also came in the days of Jehoiakim, son of Josiah, king of Judah, and continued until the eleventh year of Zedekiah, son of Josiah, king of Judah, until the people of Jerusalem were taken into exile in the fifth month of that year. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n240015|†]]</sup> ^v3

*Jeremiah’s Call and Commission*
###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> The Lord’ s message came to me, ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> “Before I formed you in your mother’s womb <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n240016|*]]</sup> I chose you. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n240017|*]]</sup> Before you were born I set you apart. I appointed you to be a prophet to the nations.” ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> I answered, “Oh, Sovereign Lord, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n240018|*]]</sup> Really <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n240019|*]]</sup> I do not know how to speak well enough for that, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400110|*]]</sup> for I am too young.” <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400111|*]]</sup> ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> The Lord said to me, “Do not say, ‘I am too young.’ But go <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400112|*]]</sup> to whomever I send you and say whatever I tell you. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Do not be afraid of those to whom I send you, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400113|*]]</sup> for I will be with you to protect <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400114|*]]</sup> you,” says the Lord. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> Then the Lord reached out his hand and touched my mouth and said to me, “I will most assuredly give you the words you are to speak for me. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400115|*]]</sup> ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> Know for certain that <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400116|*]]</sup> I hereby give you the authority to announce to nations and kingdoms that they will be <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400117|*]]</sup> uprooted and torn down, destroyed and demolished, rebuilt and firmly planted.” <sup class="nt-fn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400118|†]]</sup> ^v10

*Visions Confirming Jeremiah’s Call and Commission*
###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> Later the Lord’ s message came to me, “What do you see, Jeremiah?” I answered, “I see a branch of an almond tree.” ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> Then the Lord said, “You have observed correctly. This means <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400119|*]]</sup> I am watching to make sure my threats are carried out.” <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400120|*]]</sup> ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> The Lord’ s message came to me a second time, “What do you see?” I answered, “I see a pot of boiling water; it is tipped away from the north.” <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400121|*]]</sup> ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> Then the Lord said, “From the north <sup class="nt-fn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400122|†]]</sup> destruction will break out on all who live in the land. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> For I will soon summon all the peoples of the kingdoms of the north,” says the Lord. “They will come and their kings will set up their thrones <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400123|*]]</sup> near the entrances of the gates of Jerusalem. They will attack all the walls surrounding it and all the towns in Judah. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400125|*]]</sup> ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> In this way <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400126|*]]</sup> I will pass sentence <sup class="nt-fn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400127|†]]</sup> on the people of Jerusalem and Judah <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400128|*]]</sup> because of all their wickedness. For they rejected me and offered sacrifices to other gods, worshiping what they made with their own hands. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400129|*]]</sup> ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> “But you, Jeremiah, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400130|*]]</sup> get yourself ready! <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400131|*]]</sup> Go and tell these people everything I instruct you to say. Do not be terrified of them, or I will give you good reason to be terrified of them. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400132|*]]</sup> ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> I, the Lord, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400133|*]]</sup> hereby promise to make you <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400134|*]]</sup> as strong as a fortified city, an iron pillar, and a bronze wall. You will be able to stand up against all who live in <sup class="nt-tn">[[Jeremiah 1 — NET Notes#^n2400135|*]]</sup> the land, including the kings of Judah, its officials, its priests and all the people of the land. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> They will attack you but they will not be able to overcome you, for I will be with you to rescue you,” says the Lord. ^v19
