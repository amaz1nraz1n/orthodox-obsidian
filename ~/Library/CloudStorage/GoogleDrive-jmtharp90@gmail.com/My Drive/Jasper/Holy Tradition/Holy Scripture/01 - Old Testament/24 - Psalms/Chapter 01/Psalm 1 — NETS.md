###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> Happy the man who did not walk by the counsel of the impious, and in the way of sinners did not stand, and on the seat of pestiferous people did not sit down. ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> Rather, his will is in the law of the Lord, and on his law he will meditate day and night. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> And he will be like the tree that was planted by the channels of waters, which will yield its fruit in its season, and its leaf will not fall off. And in all that he does, he will prosper. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> Not so the impious, not so! Rather, they are like the dust that the wind flings from off the land. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> Therefore the impious will not rise up in judgment, nor sinners in the council of the righteous, ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> because the Lord knows the way of the righteous, and the way of the impious will perish. ^v6
