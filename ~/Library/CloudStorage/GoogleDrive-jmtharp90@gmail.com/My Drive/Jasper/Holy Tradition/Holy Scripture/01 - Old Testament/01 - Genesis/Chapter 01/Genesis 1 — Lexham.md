###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> In the beginning, God made the heavens and the earth. ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> But the earth was unseen and unprepared, and darkness was upon the deep. And the spirit of God rushed upon the water. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> And God said, “Let light come into being.” And light came into being. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> And God saw the light, that it was good. And God made a separation between the light and the darkness. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> And God named the light day and the darkness he named night. And there was evening, and there was morning, the first day. ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> And God said, “Let a firmament come into being in the midst of the water, and let a separator be in the midst of the water.”<sup class="nt-alt">[[Genesis 1 — Lexham Notes#^v6|⁺]]</sup> And so it happened. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> And God made the firmament, and God made a separation in the midst of the water that was under the firmament and in the midst of the water that was upon the firmament. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> And God named the firmament heaven.<sup class="nt-alt">[[Genesis 1 — Lexham Notes#^v8|⁺]]</sup> And God saw that it was good. And there was evening, and there was morning, the second day. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> And God said, “Let the water that is under the heavens be gathered together into one assembly, and let the dryness appear.” And so it happened. And the water under the heavens was brought together into their assembly, and the dryness appeared. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> And God named the dryness earth, and the assemblies of the waters he named seas. And God saw that it was good. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> And God said, “Let the earth sprout fodder plants, sowing seed according to kind and according to likeness, and fruit-bearing tree making fruit whose seed is itself in the tree, according to kind in regard to likeness upon the earth.” And so it happened. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> And the earth brought forth fodder plants, sowing seed according to kind and according to likeness, and the fruit-bearing tree making fruit whose seed is itself in the tree, corresponding to its kind upon the earth. And God saw that it was good. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> And there was evening, and there was morning, the third day. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> And God said, “Let luminaries come into being in the firmament of the heavens for illumination of the earth and rule the days and the nights and make a separation in the midst of the day and in the midst of the night. And let them be for a sign and for seasons and for days and for years. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> And let them be for illumination in the firmament of the heavens so that they might give light upon the earth.” And so it happened. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> And God made the two big luminaries, the big luminary to have authority over the day, and the little luminary to have authority over the night and the stars. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> And God placed them in the firmament of the heavens so that they might shine upon the earth ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> and rule over the day and the night and to make a separation in the midst of the light and in the midst of the darkness. And God saw that it was good. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> And there was evening, and there was morning, the fourth day. ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> And God said, “Let the water bring forth living, creeping things<sup class="nt-alt">[[Genesis 1 — Lexham Notes#^v20|⁺]]</sup> and birds flying above the earth in relation to the firmament of the heavens.” And so it happened. ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> And God made the very large fish and every life of living creeping things that the water brought forth according to their kind and every bird with wings according to kind. And God saw that it was good. ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> And God blessed them, saying, “Increase and multiply and fill the waters in the seas, and let the birds multiply upon the earth.” ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> And there was evening, and there was morning, the fifth day. ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> And God said, “Let the earth bring forth a living creature according to kind, quadrupeds and creeping things and wild animals of the earth according to kind.” And so it happened. ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> And God made the wild animals of the earth according to kind and the livestock according to kind and all the creeping things of the earth according to their kind. And God saw that it was good. ^v25

###### v26
<span class="vn">26</span> And God said, “Let us make humankind according to our image and according to our likeness, and let them rule over the fish of the sea and the birds of the heavens and the livestock and all the earth and all the creeping things that creep upon the earth.” ^v26

###### v27
<span class="vn">27</span> And God made humankind; he made him according to the image of God; he made them male and female. ^v27

###### v28
<span class="vn">28</span> And God blessed them, saying, “Increase and multiply, and fill the earth, and gain dominion over it, and rule over the fish of the sea and the birds of the heavens and all the livestock and all the earth and all the creeping things that creep upon the earth.” ^v28

###### v29
<span class="vn">29</span> And God said, “Look, I have given to you every herb that has sowable seed that is upon all the earth, and every tree that has sowable seed in its fruit will become food for you, ^v29

###### v30
<span class="vn">30</span> and for all the wild animals of the earth and for all the birds of the heavens and for every creeping thing that creeps upon the earth that has soul of life in itself. And all green fodder will be for food.” And so it happened. ^v30

###### v31
<span class="vn">31</span> And God saw everything that he made, and look, it was very good! And there was evening, and there was morning, the sixth day. ^v31
