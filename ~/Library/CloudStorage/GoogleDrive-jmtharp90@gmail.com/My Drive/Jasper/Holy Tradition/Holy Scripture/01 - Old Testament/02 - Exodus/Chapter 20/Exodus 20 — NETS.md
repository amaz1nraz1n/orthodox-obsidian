###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> And the Lord spoke all these words, saying: ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> I am the Lord your God who brought you out of the land of Egypt, out of a house of slavery. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> You shall not have other gods besides me. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> You shall not make for yourself an idol or likeness of anything whatever is in heaven above and whatever is in the earth beneath and whatever is in the waters beneath the earth. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> You shall not do obeisance to them, nor are you to serve them, for I am the Lord your God, a jealous god, repaying sins of fathers upon children up to the third and fourth generation to those who hate me, ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> and doing mercy unto thousands, for those who love me and keep my ordinances. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> You shall not take the name of the Lord your God in vain. For the Lord will never acquit the one who takes his name in vain. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Remember the day of the sabbaths to consecrate it. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> For six days you shall labor and do all your labor, ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> but on the seventh day there is Sabbata to the Lord your God. You shall not do in it any labor, you and your son and your daughter, your male slave and your female slave, your ox and your draft animal and any animal of yours and the guest who resides among you. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> For in six days the Lord made the heaven and the earth and the sea and all things in them, and he rested on the seventh day. For this reason the Lord blessed the seventh day and consecrated it. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> Honor your father and mother so that it may be well with you and so that you may be long-lived on the good land that the Lord your God is giving you. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> You shall not commit adultery. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> You shall not steal. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> You shall not murder. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> You shall not testify falsely against your neighbor with false witness. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> You shall not covet your neighbor’s wife; you shall not covet your neighbor’s house or his field or his male slave or his female slave or his ox or his draft animal or any animal of his or whatever belongs to your neighbor. ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> And all the people were perceiving the sound and the flashes and the sound of the trumpet and the mountain smoking. Now all the people were afraid and stood at a distance. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> And they said to Moyses, “You speak to us, and do not let God speak to us, lest we die.” ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> And Moyses says to them, “Take courage! For in order to test you God has come to you in order that his fear might be in you so that you do not sin.” ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> Now the people were standing at a distance, but Moyses went into the darkness where God was. ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> And the Lord said to Moyses: This is what you shall say to the house of Iakob and report to the sons of Israel, “You have seen what I have spoken from heaven to you. ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> You shall not make for yourselves silver gods, and gold gods you shall not make for yourselves. ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> An altar of earth you shall make for me, and you shall sacrifice on it your whole burnt offerings and deliverance offerings, your sheep and your bull calves in every place, there where I pronounce my name, and I will come to you and bless you. ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> But if you make an altar of stones for me, you shall not build them cut. For you have placed the tool upon it, and it has been defiled. ^v25

###### v26
<span class="vn">26</span> You shall not go up by steps onto my altar so that you do not reveal your shame upon it. ^v26
