**Author** —Nehemiah

**Date** —445 to 425 BC

**Major Theme** — *The promises of God are fulfilled through His faithful ones.*

**Background** —In the same spirit as Ezra, Nehemiah held a deep faith in God's promises. He was committed to the well-being of his fellow Jews and to the shaping of the Jewish religious community after the return from the Babylonian exile. He helped restore their hope as God's chosen people.

**Outline**

I. Return of Nehemiah and the Jewish Exiles to Jerusalem (1:1–10:39)

   A. Nehemiah's visit to Jerusalem and inspection of the wall ( 1:1–2:20 )

   B. Rebuilding of the wall ( 3:1–6:19 )

   C. Return of the exiles to Jerusalem ( 7:1–73 )

   D. Renewing the Mosaic Covenant ( 8:1–10:39 )

II. Nehemiah's Reforms in Jerusalem (11:1–13:31)
