> **Nav:** [[John 14|Hub]] · [[John 14 — EOB Notes|EOB Notes]] · [[John 14 — NET Notes|NET Notes]] · [[John 14 — Fathers|Fathers]]

###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> “Do not let your heart be troubled. Have faith in God! Have faith also in me! ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> In my Father’s house are many mansions. <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — EOB Notes#^edn1211|†]]</sup> If it were not so, I would have told you. I am going to prepare a place for you. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> If I go and prepare a place for you, I will return and receive you to myself, so that where I am, you may be there also. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> You know where I am going, and you know the way.” ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> Thomas asked him, “Lord, we do not know where you are going! And how can we know the way?” ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> Jesus replied to him, “I am the way, the truth, and the life. No one comes to the Father, except through me. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> If you had known me, you would have known my Father also. And from now on, you know him, and you have seen him.” ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Philip said to him, “Lord, show us the Father, and that will be enough for us!” ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> Jesus answered, “I have been with you for such a long time, and still, do you not know me, Philip? Whoever has seen me has seen the Father! So how can you say, ‘Show us the Father?’ ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> Do you not believe that I am in the Father, and that the Father is in me? The words that I tell you, I do not speak from myself, but the Father who lives in me accomplishes the works. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> Believe me that I am in the Father and that the Father is in me; or else believe me on account of the works themselves. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> Amen, amen, I tell you; whoever believes in me will also accomplish the works that I accomplish and will do [even] greater works than these, because I am going to my Father. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> Whatever you will ask in my Name is what I will do, so that the Father may be glorified in the Son. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> If you will ask anything in my Name, I will accomplish it. <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — EOB Notes#^edn1212|†]]</sup> ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> If you love me, keep my commandments! <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — EOB Notes#^edn1213|†]]</sup> ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> I will pray to the Father and he will give you another Counselor <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — EOB Notes#^edn1214|†]]</sup> to be with you forever, ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> the Spirit of truth. The world cannot receive him because it does not see him and does not know him. But you know him, because he lives with you and will be in you. ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> I will not leave you orphans! I will come to you! ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> In a short time, the world will no longer see me, but you will see me. Because I live, you also will live! ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> In that day, you will know that I am in my Father, and you in me, and I in you. ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> Whoever has my commandments and keeps them, that person is the one who loves me. Whoever loves me will be loved by my Father, and I will love and reveal myself to such a person.” ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> Judas (not Iscariot) asked him, “Lord, what has happened that you are about to reveal yourself to us, and not to the world?” ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> Jesus answered him, “The one who loves me will keep my word; my Father will love him and we shall come to him and make a home <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — EOB Notes#^edn1215|†]]</sup> in him. ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> Whoever does not love me does not keep <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — EOB Notes#^edn1216|†]]</sup> my words. The word which you hear is not mine, but it belongs to the Father who sent me. ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> I have said these things to you while still living with you. ^v25

###### v26
<span class="vn">26</span> But the Counselor, the Holy Spirit which the Father will send in my Name, will teach you all things and remind you of all that I said to you. ^v26

###### v27
<span class="vn">27</span> Peace I leave with you! My peace I give to you! I do not give to you as the world gives. Do not let your heart be troubled and do not let it be afraid. ^v27

###### v28
<span class="vn">28</span> You heard me say to you, ‘I am going away, and I [shall] return to you.’ If you loved me, you would have rejoiced because I said ‘I am going to my Father;’ for my Father is greater than I. <sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — EOB Notes#^edn1217|†]]</sup> ^v28

###### v29
<span class="vn">29</span> Now I have told you before it happens so that when it happens, you may believe. ^v29

###### v30
<span class="vn">30</span> I will not speak with you much longer because the ruler of the world is coming, and he has nothing in me. ^v30

###### v31
<span class="vn">31</span> But so that the world may know that I love the Father, I do exactly as the Father has commanded me. Arise, let us be on our way!” ^v31
