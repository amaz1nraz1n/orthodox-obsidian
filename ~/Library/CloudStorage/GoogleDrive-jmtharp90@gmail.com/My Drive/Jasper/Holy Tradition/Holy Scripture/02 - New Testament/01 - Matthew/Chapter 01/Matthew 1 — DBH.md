###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> The record of the lineage of Jesus the Anointed, son of David, son of Abraham: ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> Abraham sired Isaac, and Isaac sired Jacob, and Jacob sired Judah and his brothers; ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> And Judah, by Tamar, sired Perez and Zarah, and Perez sired Hezron, and Hezron sired Ram; ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> And Ram sired Amminadab, and Amminadab sired Nahshon, and Nahshon sired Salmon; ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> And Salmon, by Rahab, sired Boaz; and Boaz, by Ruth, sired Obed; and Obed sired Jesse; ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> And Jesse sired King David. David, by Uriah’s wife, sired Solomon; ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> And Solomon sired Rehoboam, and Rehoboam sired Abijah, and Abijah sired Asa; ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> And Asa sired Jehosaphat, and Jehosaphat sired Joram, and Joram sired Uzziah; ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> And Uzziah sired Jotham, and Jotham sired Ahaz, and Ahaz sired Hezekiah; ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> And Hezekiah sired Manasseh, and Manasseh sired Amos, and Amos sired Josiah; ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> And Josiah sired Jeconiah and his brothers, in the time of the removal to Babylon. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> And, after the removal to Babylon, Jeconiah sired Salathiel, and Salathiel sired Zerubbabel; ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> And Zerubbabel sired Abiud, and Abiud sired Eliakim, and Eliakim sired Azor; ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> And Azor sired Zadok, and Zadok sired Achim, and Achim sired Eliud; ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> And Eliud sired Eleazar, and Eleazar sired Matthan, and Matthan sired Jacob; ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> And Jacob sired Joseph the husband of Mary, from whom was born Jesus, called the Anointed. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> Thus there were in all fourteen generations from Abraham to David, and fourteen generations from David to the removal to Babylon, and fourteen generations from the removal to Babylon to the Anointed. ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> Now the manner of the birth of Jesus the Anointed was this: His mother Mary was betrothed to Joseph but, before they had been joined, she was discovered to be pregnant from a Holy Spirit. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> And her husband Joseph, being an upright man and not wishing to make a spectacle of her, resolved to divorce her in private. ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> But look: As he was pondering these matters the Lord’s angel appeared to him in a dream, saying, “Joseph, son of David, do not fear to receive your wife Mary; for what has been begotten within her is from a Spirit, the Holy one. ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> And she will bear a son, and you shall declare his name to be Jesus, for he will save his people from their sins. ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> All of this has occurred in order that there might be fulfilled what the Lord spoke through the prophet, saying, ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> ‘See: The virgin shall conceive in her womb and shall bear a son, and they shall declare his name to be Emmanuel’”—which, being interpreted, means “God with us.” ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> And Joseph, having arisen from sleep, did as the Lord’s angel had bidden him, and received his wife. ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> But he had no intimacy with her until she bore a son. And he declared his name to be Jesus. ^v25
