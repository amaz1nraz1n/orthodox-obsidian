###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> And it happened in the days of Achaz son of Ioatham the son of Ozias, king of Ioudas, that King Raasson of Aram and King Phakee son of Romelias of Israel went up against Ierousalem to wage war against it but could not besiege it. ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> And it was reported to the house of Dauid, saying, “Aram has made an agreement with Ephraim.” And his soul and the soul of his people were agitated as when a tree in the forest is shaken by the wind. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> Then the Lord said to Esaias, Go out to meet Achaz, you and the one who is left, your son Iasoub, at the pool on the upper road to the Fuller’s Field. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> And you will say to him, Take care to be quiet, and do not fear, nor let your soul be feeble because of these two logs of smoking firebrands, for when my fierce anger comes, I will heal again. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> And as for the son of Aram and the son of Romelias: Because they have plotted an evil counsel concerning you, saying, ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> We will go up to Judea, and after talking with them, let us turn them toward us, and we will make the son of Tabeel king over it; ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> this is what the Lord Sabaoth says: This counsel shall not remain, nor shall it come to pass. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> But the head of Aram is Damascus, but yet within sixty-five years the kingdom of Ephraim will cease from being people . ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> And the head of Ephraim is Somoron, and the head of Somoron is the son of Romelias. And if you do not believe, neither shall you understand. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> And the Lord spoke further to Achaz, saying, ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> Ask for yourself a sign of the Lord your God, in depth or in height. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> But Achaz said, I will not ask, nor will I put the Lord to the test. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> Then he said: “Hear now, O house of Dauid! Is it a small thing for you to provoke a fight with mortals? How then do you provoke a fight with the Lord? ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> Therefore the Lord himself will give you a sign. Look, the virgin shall be with child and bear a son, and you shall name him Emmanouel. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> He shall eat butter and honey; before he knows or prefers evil things, he shall choose what is good. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> For before the child knows good or bad, he defies evil to choose what is good, and the land that you fear from before the two kings will be abandoned. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> But God will bring on you and on your people and on your ancestral house such days as have not yet come since the day that he took Ephraim away from Ioudas—the king of the Assyrians.” ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> And it shall be on that day that the Lord will whistle for the flies that rule part of the river of Egypt and for the bee that is in the country of the Assyrians. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> And they will all come and rest in the ravines of the country and in the clefts of the rocks and into the caves and into every crevice and on every tree. ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> On that day the Lord will shave with the great and drunken razor—which is beyond the river of the king of the Assyrians—the head and the hair of the feet, and he will cut off the beard. ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> And it shall be on that day that a person will nourish a young cow of the cattle and two sheep, ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> and it shall be, because of the abundance of milk that they give, everyone that is left on the land will eat butter and honey. ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> And it shall be on that day that every place where there used to be a thousand vines, worth a thousand shekels, will become barren ground and thorn. ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> With dart and arrow they will enter there, for all the land will be barren ground and thorn, ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> and every hill being plowed will be plowed, and fear will not come there, for it will be turned from the barren ground and thorn to a place where a sheep can feed and an ox can tread. ^v25
