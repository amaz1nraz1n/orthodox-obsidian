###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> And this happened in the thirtieth year, in the fourth month on the fifth day of the month: I was in the middle of the captives at the Chebar River, and the heavens opened, and I saw a vision of God. ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> On the fifth day of the month; this was the fifth year of the captivity of King Jehoiakim.<sup class="nt-tn">[[Ezekiel 1 — Lexham Notes#^v2|*]]</sup> And the word of the Lord came to Ezekiel son of Buzi, the priest in the land of the Chaldeans, at the Chebar River. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> And the hand of the Lord came upon me. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> And I looked, and look! A driving spirit<sup class="nt-alt">[[Ezekiel 1 — Lexham Notes#^v4|⁺]]</sup> was coming from the north, and a great cloud was in it, and splendor was around it, and flashing fire as with lightning, and in the middle of it was something like the appearance of amber in the middle of the fire and splendor within it. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> And in the middle there was something like the likeness of four living beings, and this was the appearance of them: there was a likeness of a human upon them. ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> And each one had four faces, and each one had four wings; ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> and their legs were straight, and their feet feathered, and sparks were like flashing bronze, and their wings were light in weight. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> And underneath their wings on their four parts there was a hand of a human, and their four faces ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> did not turn about when they walked. Each one went before their face. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> And the likeness of their faces was a face of a human and a face of a lion on the right of the four, and a face of a calf on the left of the four, and a face of an eagle to the four. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> And their wings were stretched out above the four; each had two wings touching one another, and two above were covering their body. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> And each was going according to its face; wherever the spirit was going, they would go, and they did not turn. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> And in the middle of the living beings was an appearance like burning charcoal fire, like the appearance of gathered torches between the living beings, and the splendor of the fire and lightning was going forth out of the fire. ^v13

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> And I looked, and look! One wheel was upon the earth, holding the four living beings, ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> and the appearance of the wheel was like the appearance of Tarshish, and the four had one likeness. And their work was just as if a wheel was in a wheel. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> They were going upon their four parts; they were not turning while they were going, ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> and their backs were not turning, and they had height. And I saw them, and their backs were full eyes all around the four. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> And when the living beings went, the wheels holding them would go, and when the living beings lifted off from the earth, the wheels were lifted off. ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> Wherever the cloud was, there was the spirit to go; the wheels would go and be lifted off with them, because the spirit of life was in the wheels. ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> When they went, they would go, and when they stood, they would stand. And when these lifted off from the earth, they were lifted off along with them, because the spirit of life was in the wheels. ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> And a likeness above their head of the living beings was like a firmament, like the appearance of crystal stretched out above their wings. ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> And their wings were stretched out below the firmament, flapping one to another, two to each, covering their bodies. ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> And I kept hearing the sound of their wings as they went, like a sound of much water, and when they stood, their wings would stop. ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> And look! There was a voice from above the firmament that was above their head. ^v25

###### v26
<span class="vn">26</span> There was a likeness of a chair upon it, and above the likeness of the chair was a likeness as the appearance of a human from above. ^v26

###### v27
<span class="vn">27</span> And I saw what looked like an appearance of amber from the vision of the loins and up, and from the vision of the loins and down I saw what looked like fire<sup class="nt-alt">[[Ezekiel 1 — Lexham Notes#^v27|⁺]]</sup> and its splendor around about. ^v27

###### v28
<span class="vn">28</span> As the appearance of a bow when it is in the cloud on rainy days, such was the standing of the light from all around. ^v28
