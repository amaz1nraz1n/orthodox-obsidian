###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> This is the Revelation of Jesus Christ, which God gave him to show to his bondservants the things which must happen soon. He sent it and made it known by his angel <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2421|†]]</sup> to his bondservant, John, ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> who testified about everything that he saw: to the word of God and the testimony of Jesus Christ. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> Blessed is the one who reads the words of the prophecy and [blessed are] those who hear and observe <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2422|†]]</sup> the things that are written in it, for the time is at hand! ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> John, to the seven Churches that are in Asia [Minor]: May grace and peace be with you from God, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2423|†]]</sup> [the one] who is <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2424|†]]</sup> and who was and who is to come; and from the seven spirits <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2425|†]]</sup> who are before his throne; ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> and from Jesus Christ, the faithful witness, the firstborn of the dead, and the ruler of the kings of the earth. To him who loves us and washed us from our sins by his blood, ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> and made us to be a kingdom, priests <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2426|†]]</sup> to his God and Father; to him be the glory and the dominion unto the ages. Amen. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> Behold, he is coming with the clouds, and every eye will see him, including those who pierced him. All the tribes of the earth will mourn over him. It shall be so! Amen! ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> “I am the Alpha and the Omega <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2427|†]]</sup> , <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2428|†]]</sup> ” says the Lord God, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2429|†]]</sup> “who is and who was and who is to come, the Almighty.” <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2430|†]]</sup> ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> I John, your brother and partner with you in the oppression, and [in the] Kingdom, and [in] perseverance in Christ Jesus, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2431|†]]</sup> was on the island called Patmos because of the word of God and the testimony of Jesus Christ. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2432|†]]</sup> ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> I was in the Spirit on the Lord’s day <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2433|†]]</sup> and I heard behind me a loud voice, like a trumpet ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> saying, “ <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2434|†]]</sup> What you see, write [it] in a book and send [it] to the seven Churches; <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2435|†]]</sup> to Ephesus, Smyrna, Pergamum, Thyatira, Sardis, Philadelphia, and to Laodicea.” ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> I turned around to see the voice that had spoken to me. Having done so, I saw seven golden lampstands, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2436|†]]</sup> ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> and among the seven <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2437|†]]</sup> lampstands was someone like a son of man, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2438|†]]</sup> clothed with a robe reaching down to his feet, and with a golden sash <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2439|†]]</sup> around his chest. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> His head and his hair were as white as white wool, like snow. His eyes were like a flame of fire. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> His feet were like fine brass, as if it had been refined in a furnace. His voice was like the voice of many waters. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> He had seven stars in his right hand and out of his mouth came a sharp two-edged sword. His face was like the sun shining at its brightest. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> When I saw him, I fell at his feet like a dead man. He laid his right hand on me, saying, “Do not be afraid! I am the first and the last, ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> and the Living one. I was dead, and behold, I am alive unto the ages of ages! <sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — EOB Notes#^edn2440|†]]</sup> I have the keys of death and of hades. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> Therefore, write the things which you have seen, the things which are now, and the things which will happen in the future. ^v19

###### v20
