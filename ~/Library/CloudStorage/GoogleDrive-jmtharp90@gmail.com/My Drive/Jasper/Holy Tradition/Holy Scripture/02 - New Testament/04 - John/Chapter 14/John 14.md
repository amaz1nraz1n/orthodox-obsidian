*The Way to the Father*
###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> “Let not your heart be troubled; you believe in God, believe also in Me. ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> In My Father's house are many mansions;<sup class="nt-tc">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^fvar385|‡]]</sup> if it were not so, I would have told you. I go to prepare a place for you.<sup class="nt-tc">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^fvar386|‡]]</sup><sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^f4874|†]]</sup> ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> And if I go and prepare a place for you, I will come again and receive you to Myself; that where I am, there you may be also. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> And where I go you know, and the way you know.” ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> Thomas said to Him, “Lord, we do not know where You are going, and how can we know the way?” ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> Jesus said to him, “I am the way, the truth, and the life. No one comes to the Father except through Me.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^f4875|†]]</sup> ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> “If you had known Me, you would have known My Father also; and from now on you know Him and have seen Him.” ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Philip said to Him, “Lord, show us the Father, and it is sufficient for us.” ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> Jesus said to him, “Have I been with you so long, and yet you have not known Me, Philip? He who has seen Me has seen the Father; so how can you say, ‘Show us the Father’?<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^f4876|†]]</sup> ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> Do you not believe that I am in the Father, and the Father in Me? The words that I speak to you I do not speak on My own authority; but the Father who dwells in Me does the works.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^f4877|†]]</sup> ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> Believe Me that I am in the Father and the Father in Me, or else believe Me for the sake of the works themselves. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> “Most assuredly, I say to you, he who believes in Me, the works that I do he will do also; and greater works than these he will do, because I go to My Father.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^f4878|†]]</sup> ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> And whatever you ask in My name, that I will do, that the Father may be glorified in the Son.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^f4879|†]]</sup> ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> If you ask<sup class="nt-tc">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^fvar387|‡]]</sup> anything in My name, I will do it. ^v14

*The Coming of the Holy Spirit*
###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> “If you love Me, keep<sup class="nt-tc">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^fvar388|‡]]</sup> My commandments. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> And I will pray the Father, and He will give you another Helper, that He may abide with you forever—<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^f4880|†]]</sup> ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> the Spirit of truth, whom the world cannot receive, because it neither sees Him nor knows Him; but you know Him, for He dwells with you and will be in you.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^f4881|†]]</sup> ^v17

*The Son's Presence*
###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> “I will not leave you orphans; I will come to you. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> “A little while longer and the world will see Me no more, but you will see Me. Because I live, you will live also.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^f4882|†]]</sup> ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> At that day you will know that I am in My Father, and you in Me, and I in you.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^f4883|†]]</sup> ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> He who has My commandments and keeps them, it is he who loves Me. And he who loves Me will be loved by My Father, and I will love him and manifest Myself to him.” ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> Judas (not Iscariot) said to Him, “Lord, how is it that You will manifest Yourself to us, and not to the world?” ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> Jesus answered and said to him, “If anyone loves Me, he will keep My word; and My Father will love him, and We will come to him and make Our home with him. ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> He who does not love Me does not keep My words; and the word which you hear is not Mine but the Father's who sent Me. ^v24

*The Son's Departure*
###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> “These things I have spoken to you while being present with you. ^v25

###### v26
<span class="vn">26</span> But the Helper, the Holy Spirit, whom the Father will send in My name, He will teach you all things, and bring to your remembrance all things that I said to you.<sup class="nt-cit">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^fcit188|¶]]</sup> ^v26

###### v27
<span class="vn">27</span> Peace I leave with you, My peace I give to you; not as the world gives do I give to you. Let not your heart be troubled, neither let it be afraid.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^f4884|†]]</sup> ^v27

###### v28
<span class="vn">28</span> You have heard Me say to you, ‘I am going away and coming back to you.’ If you loved Me, you would rejoice because I said,<sup class="nt-tc">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^fvar389|‡]]</sup> ‘I am going to the Father,’ for My Father is greater than I.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^f4885|†]]</sup> ^v28

###### v29
<span class="vn">29</span> “And now I have told you before it comes, that when it does come to pass, you may believe.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^f4886|†]]</sup> ^v29

###### v30
<span class="vn">30</span> I will no longer talk much with you, for the ruler of this world is coming, and he has nothing in Me.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^f4887|†]]</sup> ^v30

###### v31
<span class="vn">31</span> But that the world may know that I love the Father, and as the Father gave Me commandment, so I do. Arise, let us go from here.<sup class="nt-fn">[[John 14 — OSB Notes#^f4888|†]]</sup> ^v31
