###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> Seeing the crowds, Jesus <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn76|†]]</sup> went up to the mountain and when he had sat down, his disciples came to him. ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> He began to speak and to teach them, saying: ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> Blessed are the poor <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn77|†]]</sup> in spirit,
 for theirs is the Kingdom of Heaven! <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn78|†]]</sup> ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> Blessed are those who mourn,
 for they shall be comforted! <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn79|†]]</sup> ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> Blessed are the meek,
 for they shall inherit the earth! <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn80|†]]</sup> ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> Blessed are those who hunger and thirst after righteousness,
 for they shall be filled! ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> Blessed are the merciful,
 for they shall obtain mercy! ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Blessed are the pure in heart,
 for they shall see God! ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> Blessed are the peacemakers,
 for they shall be called children <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn81|†]]</sup> of God! ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> Blessed are those who are persecuted for righteousness’ sake,
 for theirs is the Kingdom of Heaven! ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> Blessed are you when people revile you, persecute you, and say all kinds of evil against you falsely for my sake! ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> Rejoice, and be extremely glad, for great is your reward in heaven! Indeed, this is how they persecuted the prophets who were before you! ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> You are the salt of the earth, but if the salt has lost its saltiness, how can it be made salty again? It is then good for nothing, only to be cast out and trampled under foot. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> You are the light of the world! A city located on a hill <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn82|†]]</sup> cannot be hidden. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> Neither do you light a lamp and place it under a measuring basket, but on a stand. And so it shines to all who are in the house. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> Likewise, let your light shine before all, so that they may see your good works and glorify your Father who is in heaven. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> Do not think that I came to destroy the law or the prophets. I did not come to destroy, but to fulfill! ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> Amen, I tell you: until heaven and earth pass away, not even one smallest letter <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn83|†]]</sup> or one tiny pen stroke <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn84|†]]</sup> shall in any way pass away from the law, until all things are accomplished. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn85|†]]</sup> ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> Whoever, therefore, shall break one of these least commandments and teach others to do so shall be called least in the Kingdom of Heaven; but whoever shall do and teach them shall be called great in the Kingdom of Heaven. ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> Indeed, I tell you that unless your righteousness exceeds that of the scribes and Pharisees, you will by no means enter into the Kingdom of Heaven. ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> You have heard that it was said of old, ‘You shall not murder;’ <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn86|†]]</sup> and ‘Whoever commits murder shall be in danger of the judgment.’ ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> But I tell you that whoever is angry with his brother without a cause <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn87|†]]</sup> shall be in danger of the judgment. Whoever calls his brother ‘Raca!’ <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn88|†]]</sup> shall be answerable to the Sanhedrin; and whoever shall say, ‘You fool!’ shall be in danger of the fire of Gehenna. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn89|†]]</sup> ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> If therefore you are offering your gift at the altar and there remember that your brother has anything against you, ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> leave your gift there, before the altar. First, go your way and be reconciled to your brother, and then offer your gift. ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> Find an agreement with your adversary as soon as possible, even as you are on your way to court, fearing that perhaps the prosecutor will deliver you to the judge, the judge deliver you to the officer, and you may be thrown into prison. ^v25

###### v26
<span class="vn">26</span> Amen, I tell you: you will not get out of there until you have paid the last penny. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn90|†]]</sup> ^v26

###### v27
<span class="vn">27</span> You have heard that it was said to the ancients, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn91|†]]</sup> ‘You shall not commit adultery;’ <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn92|†]]</sup> ^v27

###### v28
<span class="vn">28</span> but I tell you that anyone who gazes at a woman with a view to lust <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn93|†]]</sup> after her has already committed adultery with her in his heart. ^v28

###### v29
<span class="vn">29</span> If your right eye causes you to stumble, pluck it out and throw it away from you! Indeed, it is more profitable for you that one of your members should perish than for your whole body to be cast into Gehenna. ^v29

###### v30
<span class="vn">30</span> If your right hand causes you to stumble, cut it off, and throw it away from you! It is more profitable for you that one of your members should perish, than for your whole body to be cast into Gehenna. ^v30

###### v31
<span class="vn">31</span> It was also said, ‘Whoever shall divorce <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn94|†]]</sup> his wife, let him give her a certificate of divorce,’ <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn95|†]]</sup> ^v31

###### v32
<span class="vn">32</span> but I tell you that whoever divorces his wife (except for the case of sexual immorality), makes her an adulteress; and whoever marries a woman put away in this manner commits adultery. ^v32

###### v33
<span class="vn">33</span> Again, you have heard that it was said to the people long ago, ‘You shall not swear falsely; you must fulfill your vows to the Lord.’ ^v33

###### v34
<span class="vn">34</span> But I tell you: do not swear at all! Neither by heaven, for it is the throne of God; ^v34

###### v35
<span class="vn">35</span> nor by the earth, for it is the footstool of his feet; nor by Jerusalem, for it is the city of the great King. ^v35

###### v36
<span class="vn">36</span> Do not even swear by your head because you cannot make a single hair white or black. ^v36

###### v37
<span class="vn">37</span> Instead, let your ‘Yes’ be ‘Yes’ and your ‘No’ be ‘No.’ Whatever goes beyond these is from the evil one. ^v37

###### v38
<span class="vn">38</span> You have heard that it was said, ‘An eye for an eye, and a tooth for a tooth.’ <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn96|†]]</sup> ^v38

###### v39
<span class="vn">39</span> But I tell you: do not resist one who is evil, but to whoever strikes you on your right cheek, present the other cheek as well. ^v39

###### v40
<span class="vn">40</span> If anyone sues you to take away your tunic, let him have your cloak also. ^v40

###### v41
<span class="vn">41</span> Whoever compels you to go one mile, go with him for two. ^v41

###### v42
<span class="vn">42</span> Give to whoever asks you, and do not deny whoever desires to borrow from you. ^v42

###### v43
<span class="vn">43</span> You have heard that it was said, ‘You shall love your neighbor, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn97|†]]</sup> and hate your enemy. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn98|†]]</sup> ’ ^v43

###### v44
<span class="vn">44</span> But I tell you: love your enemies, bless those who curse you, do good to those who hate you! Pray for those who mistreat you and persecute you, ^v44

###### v45
<span class="vn">45</span> so that you may be children of your Father who is in heaven. For he makes his sun to rise on the evil and the good, and he sends rain on the just and the unjust. ^v45

###### v46
<span class="vn">46</span> And so, if you love those who love you, what reward do you have? Do not even the tax collectors do the same? ^v46

###### v47
<span class="vn">47</span> If you only greet your friends, what more do you do than others? Do not even the tax collectors <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 5 — EOB Notes#^edn99|†]]</sup> do the same? ^v47

###### v48
<span class="vn">48</span> Therefore, be perfect, just as your Father in heaven is perfect. ^v48
