*Wisdom Is from the Lord*
###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> All wisdom comes from the Lord And is with Him forever.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Sirach 1 — OSB Notes#^f2762|†]]</sup> ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> Who can count the sand of the seas, The drops of rain, and the days of eternity? ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> Who can search out the height of heaven, The breadth of the earth, the abyss, and wisdom? ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> Wisdom was created before all things, And the insight of prudence was from eternity.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Sirach 1 — OSB Notes#^f2763|†]]</sup> ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> To whom has the root of wisdom been revealed? And who has come to know her great deeds? ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> There is one who is wise and is feared exceedingly, He who sits upon His throne.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Sirach 1 — OSB Notes#^f2764|†]]</sup> ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> The Lord Himself created wisdom. He saw and numbered her And poured her out on all His works,<sup class="nt-fn">[[Sirach 1 — OSB Notes#^f2765|†]]</sup> ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> In the midst of all flesh according to His gift; And He provided her for those who love Him. ^v8

*The Fear of the Lord*
###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> The fear of the Lord is glory and boasting, And gladness and a crown of rejoicing. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> The fear of the Lord will cheer the heart And will give gladness, joy, and long life.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Sirach 1 — OSB Notes#^f2766|†]]</sup> ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> For those who fear the Lord, it shall be well to the utmost, And on the day of his death, he will be blessed. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> The beginning of wisdom is to fear the Lord, And she was joined with the faithful in the womb.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Sirach 1 — OSB Notes#^f2767|†]]</sup> ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> She constructed a foundation of life among men And will be trusted among their seed. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> The gratification of wisdom is to fear the Lord, And she intoxicates them with her fruits. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> She will fill every house of theirs with objects of desire And their storehouses with her harvest. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> The fear of the Lord is the crown of wisdom, Making peace and soundness of health to flourish. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> The Lord saw and numbered her, And poured out the power of comprehension; And He exalted the glory of those who hold fast to her. ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> The root of wisdom is to fear the Lord, And her branches are length of days. ^v18

*Warnings to the Wise*
###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> Unjust anger cannot be justified, For anger's decisive influence causes his fall. ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> A patient man will hold fast until the proper time, Then afterwards gladness shall burst forth for him. ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> He shall conceal his words until the proper time, And the lips of many will tell of his understanding. ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> In the treasures of wisdom are the parables of knowledge, But godliness is an abomination to a sinner. ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> If you desire wisdom, keep the commandments, And the Lord will supply it to you.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Sirach 1 — OSB Notes#^f2768|†]]</sup> ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> For the fear of the Lord is wisdom and instruction, And His good pleasure is faith and gentleness. ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> Do not disobey the fear of the Lord, And do not come to Him with a divided heart. ^v25

###### v26
<span class="vn">26</span> Do not be a hypocrite in the sight of men, And be careful with your lips. ^v26

###### v27
<span class="vn">27</span> Do not exalt yourself, lest you fall And bring dishonor to your soul. The Lord shall reveal your secrets, And in the midst of the assembly He will strike you down, Because you did not come in the fear of the Lord And your heart was full of deceit. ^v27
