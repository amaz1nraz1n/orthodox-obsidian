###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> This is a psalm written with David's own hand, although outside the number, when he fought in single combat with Goliath.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Psalms 151 — OSB Notes#^f2128|†]]</sup>Iwas small among my brothers And the youngest in my father's house; I tended my father's sheep. ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> My hands built a musical instrument; My fingers tuned a lyre. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> And who shall tell my Lord? The Lord Himself, He Himself hears. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> He sent forth His Angel And took me from my father's sheep; And he anointed me with his anointing oil. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> My brothers were handsome and tall, But the Lord took no pleasure in them. ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> I went out to meet the foreigner, And he cursed me with his idols; ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> But I drew his own sword and beheaded him, And removed disgrace from the children of Israel. ^v7
