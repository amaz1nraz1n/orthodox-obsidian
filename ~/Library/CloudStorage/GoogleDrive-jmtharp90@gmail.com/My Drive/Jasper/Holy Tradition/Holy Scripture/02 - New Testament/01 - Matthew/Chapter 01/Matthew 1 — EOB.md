###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> The book of the origins <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — EOB Notes#^edn29|†]]</sup> of Jesus Christ, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — EOB Notes#^edn30|†]]</sup> the son of David, the son of Abraham. ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> Abraham became the father of Isaac. Isaac became the father of Jacob. Jacob became the father of Judah and his brothers. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> Judah became the father of Perez and Zerah by Tamar. Perez became the father of Hezron. Hezron became the father of Ram. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> Ram became the father of Amminadab. Amminadab became the father of Nahshon. Nahshon became the father of Salmon. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> Salmon became the father of Boaz by Rahab. Boaz became the father of Obed by Ruth. Obed became the father of Jesse. ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> Jesse became the father of King David. David became the father of Solomon by her who had been the wife of Uriah. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> Solomon became the father of Rehoboam. Rehoboam became the father of Abijah. Abijah became the father of Asa. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — EOB Notes#^edn31|†]]</sup> ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Asa became the father of Jehoshaphat. Jehoshaphat became the father of Joram. Joram became the father of Uzziah. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> Uzziah became the father of Jotham. Jotham became the father of Ahaz. Ahaz became the father of Hezekiah. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> Hezekiah became the father of Manasseh. Manasseh became the father of Amon. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — EOB Notes#^edn32|†]]</sup> Amon became the father of Josiah. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> Josiah became the father of Jechoniah and his brothers, at the time of the exile to Babylon. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> After the exile to Babylon, Jechoniah became the father of Shealtiel. Shealtiel became the father of Zerubbabel. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> Zerubbabel became the father of Abiud. Abiud became the father of Eliakim. Eliakim became the father of Azor. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> Azor became the father of Sadoc. Sadoc became the father of Achim. Achim became the father of Eliud. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> Eliud became the father of Eleazar. Eleazar became the father of Matthan. Matthan became the father of Jacob. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> Jacob became the father of Joseph, the husband of Mary, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — EOB Notes#^edn33|†]]</sup> of whom was born Jesus who is called Christ. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> And so, all the generations from Abraham to David are fourteen generations; from David to the exile to Babylon fourteen generations; and from the exile to Babylon until the Christ, fourteen generations. ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> Now the birth of Jesus Christ happened like this: after his mother, Mary, was promised in marriage to Joseph but before they came together, she was found pregnant by the Holy Spirit. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> Joseph, her betrothed, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — EOB Notes#^edn34|†]]</sup> who was a righteous man did not want to make her a public spectacle and so intended to put her away <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — EOB Notes#^edn35|†]]</sup> quietly. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — EOB Notes#^edn36|†]]</sup> ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> But as he was thinking about these things, behold, an angel of the Lord appeared to him in a dream, saying, “Joseph, son of David, do not be afraid to take Mary your wife home, for what is conceived in her is by the Holy Spirit. ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> She will give birth to a son and you shall give him the name Jesus <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — EOB Notes#^edn37|†]]</sup> because he will be the one to save his people from their sins.” ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> Now all this happened so that what had been spoken by the Lord through the prophet might be fulfilled: ^v22

###### v23
<span class="vn">23</span> Behold, the virgin shall be with child and bring forth a son.
 They shall call his name Emmanuel;
 which means, ‘with us [is] God.’ <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — EOB Notes#^edn38|†]]</sup> ^v23

###### v24
<span class="vn">24</span> Joseph arose from his sleep and did as the angel of the Lord had commanded him: he took his wife to [live with] him, ^v24

###### v25
<span class="vn">25</span> and had no relations with her before <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — EOB Notes#^edn39|†]]</sup> she had brought forth her firstborn <sup class="nt-fn">[[Matthew 1 — EOB Notes#^edn40|†]]</sup> son; and he named him Jesus. ^v25
