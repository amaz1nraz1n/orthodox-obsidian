*The Golden Image*
###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> In his eighteenth year, King Nebuchadnezzar made a golden image. Its height was sixty cubits and its width, six cubits, and he set it up in the plain of Dura, in the province of Babylon.<sup class="nt-fn">[[Daniel 3 — OSB Notes#^f4013|†]]</sup><sup class="nt-lit">[[Daniel 3 — OSB Notes#^fx220|☩]]</sup> ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> Then Nebuchadnezzar sent to gather together the high officials, the commanders, the governors, the rulers, and all those in authority, along with all the rulers of the provinces, to come to the dedication of the image King Nebuchadnezzar set up. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> So the governors, the high officials, the leaders, the great rulers—all those in authority who ruled the provinces came to the dedication of the image King Nebuchadnezzar set up, and they stood before the image Nebuchadnezzar made. ^v3

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> but does not fall down and worship the golden image shall be cast into the burning fiery furnace. ^v11

###### v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> Then Nebuchadnezzar, in rage and fury, gave the command to bring Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego. So the men were brought before the king. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> Nebuchadnezzar then answered and said to them, “Is it true, Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego, that you do not serve my gods or worship the golden image I set up? ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> Now then, if you are ready, when you hear the sound of the trumpet, the pipe, the harp, the four-stringed instrument, the psaltery, the symphony, and every kind of music, that you shall fall down and worship the golden image I made. But indeed, if you do not worship it at that time, you shall be cast into the burning fiery furnace. Then what god is there who will deliver you from my hands?” ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego answered and said to King Nebuchadnezzar, “We have no need to answer you in regard to this thing. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> For there is a God in the heavens, whom we serve, and He is able to save us from the burning fiery furnace; and He will deliver us from your hands, O king. ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> But if not, let it be known to you, O king, that we will not serve your gods, nor worship the golden image you set up.” ^v18

###### v40
<span class="vn">40</span> So let this be our sacrifice before You today, And may it be accomplished for those who follow You; For there is no shame for those who trust in You. ^v40

###### v41
<span class="vn">41</span> Now we are following You with all our heart, And we fear You and seek Your face. ^v41

###### v42
<span class="vn">42</span> “Do not put us to shame, But deal with us according to Your kindness And according to the abundance of Your mercy. ^v42

###### v43
<span class="vn">43</span> Deliver us by Your wondrous works And give glory to Your name, O Lord. ^v43

###### v44
<span class="vn">44</span> May all those who inflict evils upon Your servants Be put to shame and humiliated in their power; And let their strength be crushed. ^v44

###### v45
<span class="vn">45</span> Let them know that You alone are the Lord God And glorious over all the inhabited earth.” ^v45

###### v46

*The King Praises God*
###### v91
<span class="vn">91</span> Now Nebuchadnezzar heard their singing, and he marveled and rose up in haste, and said to his nobles, “Did we not cast three men bound into the fire?” They replied to the king, “Truly, O king.”<sup class="nt-fn">[[Daniel 3 — OSB Notes#^f4020|†]]</sup> ^v91

###### v92
<span class="vn">92</span> Then the king said, “Behold, I see four men untied and walking in the midst of the fire, yet they are not destroyed; and the vision of the fourth is like the Son of God.” ^v92

###### v93
<span class="vn">93</span> Then the king approached the door of the burning fiery furnace, and called out to them by name, “Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego, servants of the Most High God, come forth and come here!” So Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego came forth from the midst of the fire. ^v93

###### v94
<span class="vn">94</span> Then the governors, the commanders, the viceroys, and the court officials gathered together and beheld the men, that the fire had no power over their bodies; neither had it singed their hair, nor scorched their clothes, nor was the smell of fire on them. ^v94

###### v95
<span class="vn">95</span> So King Nebuchadnezzar answered and said, “Blessed is the God of Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego, who sent His Angel and saved His servants who trusted in Him; for they altered the word of the king and handed over their bodies to be burned, so as not to serve and worship any god other than their God. ^v95

###### v96
<span class="vn">96</span> Therefore I make a decree: ‘Any people, tribe, or language that blasphemes the God of Shadrach, Meshech, and Abednego shall be destroyed, and their houses plundered, because there is no other God who can deliver in this way.’ ” ^v96

###### v97
