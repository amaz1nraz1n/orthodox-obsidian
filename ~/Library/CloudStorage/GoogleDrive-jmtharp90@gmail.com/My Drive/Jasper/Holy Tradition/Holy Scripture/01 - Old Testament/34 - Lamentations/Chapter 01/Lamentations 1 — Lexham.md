###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> How the city that had been filled with people sits alone! She has become like a widow. Filled with nations, a princess among the territories has become tribute. ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> Weeping, she wept at night, and her tears were upon her cheeks. And of all those who loved her, there was no one to comfort her. All who loved her dealt treacherously with her; they became enemies to her. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> Judea was resettled because of her lowliness, and from the magnitude of her slavery. She sat among the nations; she has not found rest. All those who pursue her have seized her between those who afflict. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> The ways of Zion mourn, for there is no one to come to a feast. All her gates are destroyed; her priests groan. Her virgins are carried away, and she is embittered in herself. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> Those who afflict her have become a head, and her enemies are prospering because the Lord humbled her at the number of her ungodly acts. Her children were brought into captivity from the face of an oppressor. ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> And all her dignity has been removed from the daughter of Zion. Her rulers have become like rams not finding pasturage, and they kept going in no strength at the face of the pursuer. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> Jerusalem remembered the days of her lowliness and her rejection and all her desirable things that were from the days of old, when her people fell into the hands of oppressors, and there was no one to help her. Seeing this, her enemies laughed at her dwelling. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Jerusalem sinned a sin. Therefore, she has become restless. All who extolled her have brought her low, for they saw her shame. And surely, she is groaning; and she was turned away backward. ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> Her uncleanness was from her feet; she did not remember her extremities. And she brought down swollen things; there is none who comforts her. See, O Lord, my lowliness, that an enemy was magnified. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> The oppressor spread out his hand over all her desired objects, for she saw nations enter into her sanctuary, whom you commanded not to enter into your assembly. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> All her people sigh, seeking bread; they gave their desires for food for their soul to return. See, O Lord, and look, for she has become dishonored. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> As for all those who go beside you, on the road, turn about and see whether there is pain according to my pain that has happened. Speaking with respect to me, the Lord made me low on the day of the wrath of his anger. ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> From his height he sent fire; he brought it down into my bones. He spread a net for my feet; he turned me back. He granted me to be removed, sorrowing the whole day. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> He was watchful over my profane acts; they were woven by my hands; they went up on my neck. My strength became weak because the Lord gave pain into my hands. I will not be able to stand. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> The Lord removed all my mighty men out of the middle of me; he proclaimed against me a time to crush my choicest men. The Lord has trod the winepress for the virgin daughter of Judah; I lament at these things. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> My eye has brought down water because the one who comforts me is distant from me, the one who returns my life. My sons became removed because the enemy was strengthened. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> Zion spread out her hands; there is no one to comfort her. The Lord commanded Jacob; those who are afflicting him are all around him. Jerusalem became a woman who sits apart between them. ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> The Lord is righteous, because I have provoked his mouth. Hear now, all peoples, and see my pain. My virgins and my young men have gone into captivity. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> I called to my lovers, but they deceived me. My priests and my elders in the city came to an end because they sought food for themselves in order to restore their souls, and they did not find any. ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> See, O Lord, that I am afflicted; my belly is stirred up, and my heart has turned within me because, provoked, I have been provoked. Outside, a sword has made childless, just as death at home. ^v20

###### v21
<span class="vn">21</span> Hear now, for I am groaning; there is no one to comfort me. All my enemies have heard my calamities, and they rejoiced, for you have acted. You have brought on the day; you have proclaimed the time; they have become like me. ^v21

###### v22
<span class="vn">22</span> May all their evil go in at your face, and glean them, the way they have made a gleaning about all my sins; for my groanings are many, and my heart is grieved. ^v22
