###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> And the Lord summoned Moyses and spoke to him from the tent of witness, saying: ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> Speak to the sons of Israel, and you shall say to them: When a person of you brings gifts of livestock to the Lord, you shall bring your gifts from the cattle and from the sheep. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> If his gift is a whole burnt offering from the cattle, he shall present a male without blemish; he shall bring it to the door of the tent of witness, acceptable before the Lord. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> And he shall lay his hand on the head of the offering, a thing acceptable to him to atone for him. ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> And they shall slaughter the bull calf before the Lord, and the sons of Aaron the priests shall offer the blood, and they shall pour out the blood against all sides of the altar that is at the entrance of the tent of witness. ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> And when they flay the whole burnt offering, they shall cut it up limb by limb. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> And the sons of Aaron the priests shall put fire on the altar and pile wood onto the fire. ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> And the sons of Aaron the priests shall pile the divided parts and the head and the suet onto the wood that is on the fire on the altar, ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> but the entrails and the legs they shall wash with water, and the priests shall lay everything on the altar. It is an offering, a sacrifice, an odor of fragrance to the Lord. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> But if his gift for a whole burnt offering to the Lord is from the small cattle, both from the lambs and the kids, he shall present a male without blemish, and he shall lay his hand on its head. ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> And they shall slaughter it on the north side of the altar before the Lord, and the sons of Aaron the priests shall pour out its blood against all sides of the altar. ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> And they shall divide it up limb by limb with its head and its suet, and the priests shall pile them on the wood that is on the fire on the altar, ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> and its entrails and its legs they shall wash with water. And the priest shall present everything and lay it on the altar. It is an offering, a sacrifice, an odor of fragrance to the Lord. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> But if the gift he brings to the Lord is an offering from the birds, then he shall bring his gift from the turtledoves or from the doves. ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> And the priest shall do the presenting at the altar and snip off the head. And the priest shall do the placing on the altar and squeeze out the blood against the base of the altar. ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> And he shall remove the crop with the feathers and throw it at the east side of the altar, in the place for ashes. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> And he shall break it open by the wings and not divide it. And the priest shall lay it on the altar on the wood that is on the fire. It is an offering, a sacrifice, an odor of fragrance to the Lord. ^v17
