### [[Wisdom of Solomon 1#v1|1:1, 2]]
^v1
> [!note] 1:1, 2 ^f2655
> Jesus promised, “**Seek**, and you will find” ([[Matthew 7#v7|Mt 7:7]]), just as is promised here.

### [[Wisdom of Solomon 1#v4|1:4]]
^v4
> [!note] 1:4 ^f2656
> According to St. Paul, Christ is “the **wisdom** of God” ([[I Corinthians 1#v24|1Co 1:24]]).

### [[Wisdom of Solomon 1#v5|1:5]]
^v5
> [!note] 1:5 ^f2657
> A **spirit of discipline** brings with it honesty, discernment, and righteousness.

### [[Wisdom of Solomon 1#v7|1:7]]
^v7
> [!note] 1:7 ^f2658
> This passage brings to mind the prayer, “O Heavenly King,” in which we confess, “the **Spirit** of Truth . . . is everywhere present and **fills** all things.”

### [[Wisdom of Solomon 1#v13|1:13, 14]]
^v13
> [!note] 1:13, 14 ^f2659
> God intends for man to have eternal life. Evil and **death** are not of God's making (see [[James 1#v12|Jam 1:12–18]]).
