### [[Jeremiah 1#v1|1:1]]
^v1
> [!note] 1:1 ^f3588
> **Jeremiah** means “God appoints or exalts.” **Anathoth** is his hometown, within the **land of Benjamin**.

### [[Jeremiah 1#v2|1:2-3]]
^v2
> [!note] 1:2-3 ^f3589
> Jeremiah prophesied during the reigns of **Josiah**,** Jehoiakim**,** **and** Zedekiah**.

### [[Jeremiah 1#v5|1:5]]
^v5
> [!note] 1:5 ^f3590
> God's foreknowledge and His calling are here woven together.

### [[Jeremiah 1#v6|1:6]]
^v6
> [!note] 1:6 ^f3591
> Like Moses before him ([[Exodus 4#v10|Ex 4:10]]), Jeremiah claims he does not **know how to speak**. As with all the prophets, God put His words in Jeremiah's mouth (v. [[Jeremiah 1#v9|9]]).

### [[Jeremiah 1#v16|1:16]]
^v16
> [!note] 1:16 ^f3592
> Note the three sins of Judah, and of all mankind: (1) forsaking God; (2) false worship; and (3) preoccupation with themselves.
