*Psalm 51 __NOTE_n190511__*
###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> Have mercy on me, O God, because of <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n190513|*]]</sup> your loyal love. Because of <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n190514|*]]</sup> your great compassion, wipe away my rebellious acts. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n190515|*]]</sup> ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> Wash away my wrongdoing. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n190516|*]]</sup> Cleanse me of my sin. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n190517|†]]</sup> ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> For I am aware of <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n190518|*]]</sup> my rebellious acts; I am forever conscious of my sin. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n190519|*]]</sup> ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> Against you—you above all <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905110|*]]</sup> —I have sinned; I have done what is evil in your sight. So <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905111|*]]</sup> you are just when you confront me; <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905112|*]]</sup> you are right when you condemn me. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905113|*]]</sup> ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> Look, I was guilty of sin from birth, a sinner the moment my mother conceived me. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905114|*]]</sup> ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> Look, <sup class="nt-fn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905115|†]]</sup> you desire <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905116|*]]</sup> integrity in the inner man; <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905117|*]]</sup> you want me to possess wisdom. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905118|*]]</sup> ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> Cleanse me <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905119|*]]</sup> with hyssop <sup class="nt-fn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905120|†]]</sup> and I will be pure; <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905121|*]]</sup> wash me <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905122|*]]</sup> and I will be whiter than snow. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905123|†]]</sup> ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> Grant me the ultimate joy of being forgiven. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905124|*]]</sup> May the bones <sup class="nt-fn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905125|†]]</sup> you crushed rejoice. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905126|*]]</sup> ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> Hide your face <sup class="nt-fn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905127|†]]</sup> from my sins. Wipe away <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905128|*]]</sup> all my guilt. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> Create for me a pure heart, O God. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905129|†]]</sup> Renew a resolute spirit within me. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905130|*]]</sup> ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> Do not reject me. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905131|*]]</sup> Do not take your holy Spirit <sup class="nt-fn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905132|†]]</sup> away from me. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905133|†]]</sup> ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> Let me again experience the joy of your deliverance. Sustain me by giving me the desire to obey. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905134|*]]</sup> ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> Then I will teach <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905135|*]]</sup> rebels your merciful ways, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905136|*]]</sup> and sinners will turn <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905137|*]]</sup> to you. ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> Rescue me from the guilt of murder, <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905138|*]]</sup> O God, the God who delivers me. Then my tongue will shout for joy because of your righteousness. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905139|*]]</sup> ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> O Lord, give me the words. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905140|*]]</sup> Then my mouth will praise you. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905141|*]]</sup> ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> Certainly <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905142|*]]</sup> you do not want a sacrifice, or else I would offer it; <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905143|*]]</sup> you do not desire a burnt sacrifice. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905144|†]]</sup> ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> The sacrifice God desires is a humble spirit <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905145|*]]</sup> — O God, a humble and repentant heart <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905146|*]]</sup> you will not reject. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905147|*]]</sup> ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> Because you favor Zion, do what is good for her. <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905148|*]]</sup> Fortify <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905149|*]]</sup> the walls of Jerusalem. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> Then you will accept <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905151|*]]</sup> the proper sacrifices, burnt sacrifices and whole offerings; then bulls will be sacrificed <sup class="nt-tn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905152|*]]</sup> on your altar. <sup class="nt-fn">[[Psalms 50 — NET Notes#^n1905153|†]]</sup> ^v19
