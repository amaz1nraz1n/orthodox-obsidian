###### v1
<span class="vn">1</span> A revelation from Jesus the Anointed, which God gave him, to show his slaves what things must occur extremely soon, and he signified this by sending it out through his angel to his slave John, ^v1

###### v2
<span class="vn">2</span> Who attested to the word of God and the witness of Jesus the Anointed, regarding as many things as he saw. ^v2

###### v3
<span class="vn">3</span> How blissful both that lector of and those listeners to the prophecy who also abide by the things written therein; for the time is near. ^v3

###### v4
<span class="vn">4</span> John to the seven assemblies that are in Asia: grace and peace to you from the one who is and who was and who is to come, and from the seven Spirits that are before his throne, ^v4

###### v5
<span class="vn">5</span> And from Jesus the Anointed, the faithful witness, the firstborn of the dead, and the Archon of the kings of the earth. To the one who has loved us and has loosed us from our sins by his blood, ^v5

###### v6
<span class="vn">6</span> And has made us into a Kingdom, priests to his God and Father—to him be the glory and the might unto the ages of the ages; amen. ^v6

###### v7
<span class="vn">7</span> Look: He is coming with the clouds, and every eye will see him, and they who pierced him and all the tribes of the earth will beat their breasts in lamentation because of him. Yes, amen! ^v7

###### v8
<span class="vn">8</span> “I am the alpha and the omega,” says the Lord God, “the one who is and who was and who is coming, the Almighty.” ^v8

###### v9
<span class="vn">9</span> I, John, your brother and fellow communicant in the affliction and Kingdom and endurance in Jesus, happened to be on the island called Patmos for the sake of God’s word and of witnessing to Jesus. ^v9

###### v10
<span class="vn">10</span> On the Lord’s day I happened to be in spirit and I heard a voice as loud as a trumpet behind me, ^v10

###### v11
<span class="vn">11</span> Saying, “Write what you see in a book and send it to the seven assemblies—to Ephesus and to Smyrna and to Pergamum and to Thyatira and to Sardis and to Philadelphia and to Laodicea.” ^v11

###### v12
<span class="vn">12</span> And I turned to look toward the voice that spoke to me; and, having turned, I saw seven gold lampstands, ^v12

###### v13
<span class="vn">13</span> And amid the lampstands one like a son of man<sup class="nt-fn">[[Revelation 1 — DBH Notes#^fn54|†]]</sup> garbed down to his feet and girdled around at the chest with a golden cincture, ^v13

###### v14
<span class="vn">14</span> And his head and hair were as white as wool—a white like snow—and his eyes like flames of fire, ^v14

###### v15
<span class="vn">15</span> And his feet like fine brass, as if fired in a furnace, and his voice was like a sound of many waters, ^v15

###### v16
<span class="vn">16</span> And he held seven stars in his right hand, and a sharp two-edged sword coming out of his mouth, and his face was like the sun shining at full strength. ^v16

###### v17
<span class="vn">17</span> And when I saw him I fell down at his feet like a dead man; and he placed his right hand on me, saying, “Do not be afraid: I am the first and the last, ^v17

###### v18
<span class="vn">18</span> And the one who lives and who became dead and—see!—I am alive unto the ages of the ages, and I have the keys of death and of Hades. ^v18

###### v19
<span class="vn">19</span> Therefore, write down the things you have seen and the things that are and the things about to occur thereafter. ^v19

###### v20
<span class="vn">20</span> The mystery of the seven stars you saw upon my right hand and the seven gold lampstands—the seven stars are angels of the seven assemblies, and the seven lampstands are seven assemblies. ^v20
